# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users

Path of Exile players who compare, tune, and track builds while planning their next progression step.

## Product Purpose

PoE Build Lab turns a build's equipment, damage, survivability, and upgrade options into one focused workspace.

## Positioning

An expert build-planning surface that makes dense Path of Exile decisions easier to inspect and act on.

## Brand Personality

Precise, capable, and quietly arcane.

## Anti-references

Avoid medieval-game pastiche, generic SaaS cards, and loud fantasy effects.

## Design Principles

- Keep build signals visible without overwhelming the player.
- Make every colored state carry useful meaning.
- Treat item inspection as a focused, information-rich task.
- Use Path of Exile references as texture, not costume.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Keyboard-operable controls, strong contrast, visible focus states, and reduced-motion support.
