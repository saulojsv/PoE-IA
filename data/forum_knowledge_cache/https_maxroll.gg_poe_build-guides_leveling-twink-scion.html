Post-Campaign

If you are not level 60 by the time you finish Act 10, stay in the Blood Aqueducts in Act 9 for more EXP. Typically players respec their twink leveling over to the build of their choice once entering maps, or around level 70. This is the level bracket which allows you to equip level 20 Skill Gems, as well as being able to equip most end-game gear.

After completing the Campaign, there are two standard routes of leveling your character.

  • Farm White Maps and kill monsters yourself
  • Farm Breachstones, often by having someone carry you

Farming White Maps

The most straight forward way of leveling up is by Farming White Maps. Farming white maps is virtually free, but it is less efficient than Breachstones or getting carried. Just use an Orb of Alchemy on Tier 1 maps, slowly working your way up.

Breachstones

Putting a Xoph's Breachstone, Esh's Breachstone, or Tul's Breachstone into the Map device once you switch your build over provides a massive amount of experience at a fairly cheap cost. The biggest downside of this strategy is that these can be scarily dangerous, and some builds struggle to complete these. If this is the case, it is recommended to see if a friend, guild mate, or someone else to help you with a few Breachstones. To incentivize the other person you can say something like "my EXP your loot."

The old strategy of Breachstone Rota's (Rotations), is mostly dead thanks. If you wish, you can still buy a Pure Breachstone and attempt to find 5 other people to each contribute one Pure Breachstone for a rotation. One good place to find other players for this is typing /global 820 in game. A Rota means you only have to pay for one Breachstone, but you get the EXP from 6. Do not do Pure Breachstone Rota's below level 72.

Summary

  • Twink Scion Leveling with Hollow Palm is zoom zoom fast fast gogo pew pew.
  • One With Nothing, Astramentis, and Call of the Void are essential items to have to get the job done.
  • Once you finish the campaign farm maps, breaches, or get carried to level up.
