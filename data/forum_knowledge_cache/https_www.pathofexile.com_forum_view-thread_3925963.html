Shield Crush Slayer / Gladiator

I'm trying to put all this together into one pob for y'all

Level 31 0 Chaos : https://pobb.in/5irChbhsH46J

level 76 0 chaos : https://pobb.in/ch7XjrN5ynsT

At this point we transition to filling out the atlas, use the strongbox and scarab nodes and whatever else you enjoy, personally I feel like kingsmarch is a trap at this point

Level 84 2 chaos : https://pobb.in/0nBCcJSHT-IP

We're still on a 5 link but upgraded the weapon to a 3 green socket prismatic eclipse in order to increase our attack speed

level 93 20 chaos : https://pobb.in/Rq_7SV4Iqbzl

2/3rd of the atlas, still on a 5 link

level 95 200 chaos : https://pobb.in/KzSo5iwAroiJ

All voidstones gotten

level 98 2000 chaos : https://pobb.in/8BCBLS3EIwMh

At this point transition to farming uber atziri, it is purely mechanical and you have the dps for it, she drops several things you will want or want to sell. You should be able to fight her 8-10 times per hour, your drops will vary, but she has multiple big ticket items. The one you're after is the div card so you can gamble it, or just take your profit per hour and sell it to buy the belt you want.

Level 100 200,000 chaos

https://pobb.in/hb4SmAhgx3jA

At this point we're going to transition to farming simulacrums in order to get the next jewels we want. You'll notice belt occupies the bulk of the cost.

level 100 800,000 chaos https://pobb.in/wT9hxeTVtIz7

You may need to farm 2,000 simulacrums to get here, but it will eventually drop, or you can sell your voices unided and just save up for the one you want over time.

At this stage the only thing left to do for the build is to buy temples with corruption chambers and uncorrupted versions of your jewels and gloves in order to attempt double corruptions on those items. Like that Seething Fury.

Prices are roughly current for march 31st 2026 in the Mirage Softcore league, divines currently at 296 chaos each, mirrors at 900 divines or 266,400 chaos. I assembled it because its a shield crush build I found on poe.ninja and its progross roughly followed logarithmic growth, which is to say powers of 10 as the days went on. I hope this helps people fill in the rest of the owl for shield crush builds floating around. The 2000 chaos version is also tested down to 800 depth with some variation : https://pobb.in/5k-Y1xJytkwY || https://poe.ninja/poe1/builds/mirage/character/Heebboo-5777/Heeb_DeepBoy?type=depthsolo&i=1&search=type%3Ddepthsolo%26class%3DSlayer%26skills%3DShield%2BCrush

So does anybody else have any questions, or tips for people taking advantage of the guide? Did framing it with those milestones help you?
