Holy Flame Totem Hierophant League Starter for Path of Exile Mirage 3.28

by aer0
Mirage 3.28Last Updated: March 24, 2026

Advertisement

This website is supported by ads.

The Holy Flame Totem Hierophant League Starter provides a balance of damage, clear and defence on a budget. The Holy Flame Totem shoots fire projectiles in a massive cone, handling large packs and bosses with ease.

The Hierophant ascendancy offers massive damage and recovery through Ritual of Awakening, rewarding you for each totem you place. Dropping Searing Bond totems as you go keeps your damage and recovery maxed out. Meanwhile, Arcane Blessing provides a massive boost to damage thanks to the build's Arcane Surge scaling.

Huge Mana and Life recovery allow for a comfortable playstyle alongside Block and Spell Block. Place your Totems and continue to move, dodging boss attacks and spells while your Totems continue to rain fire on your enemies.

Resistances

You do not need specific resistances on any item, just get the required total resistances amongst all gear combined. You need to cap your Resistances at 75% unless otherwise specified in the guide!

Skills

Endgame Skills
  • Grab Holy Flame Totem at level 4 and enjoy using your endgame skill right away! Early on, you need to supplement your Totem's damage with your own damage, so self cast Purifying Flame. Stand on the Consecrated Ground created by both skills for a significant amount of Life Regeneration.
  • As Holy Flame Totem levels, it gains projectiles. This results in a wider cone of damage. In order for the projectiles to travel further, acquire Projectile Speed, mostly notably from the gem's quality.
  • Once you hit Level 16, drop Purifying Flame and supplement your damage with Wave of Conviction mines via Blastchain Mine Support. Mines are required because Ancestral Bond prevents you from dealing damage yourself. These mines also grant Exposure, reducing enemy resistance.
  • Place a Flame Wall between the totems and the enemy for extra added damage.
  • Your early game auras are Herald of Ash, Herald of Purity and Clarity. Keep Clarity at level 5 to prevent it from requiring too much reservation.
  • In order to maximize your recovery and damage from Ritual of Awakening, place Searing Bond Totems as you move around to ensure you are at maximum totems at all times. These utility Totems are not designed to deal damage, rather they get placed anywhere around you to increase the value of the Ascendancy.
    • NOTE: Keep in mind these can overwrite your Holy Flame Totem, so always ensure you have your maximum up!
  • Replace Wave of Conviction with Pyroclast Mine of Sabotage for even more resistance reduction on bosses. Use these mines to apply the aura, but you can also detonate one from time to time to apply the debuff from Combustion Support. Detonating the mines also provides Power and Frenzy charges thanks to Charged Mines Support.
  • Once you have the Anathema ring, you can apply a number of curses equal to your maximum Power Charges. Use Arcanist Brand to cast them early on. In the endgame, craft a helmet with the suffix "Trigger Socketed Spells when you Focus, with a 0.25 second Cooldown" in order to automatically cast all the curses in your Helmet.

Ascendancy

How to Ascend

Learn how to find the Trials of Ascendancy and complete The Lord's Labyrinth to earn your Ascensions with our Labyrinth Guide!

  • Pursuit of Faith grants an additional totem for a large boost to damage. The additional totem placement speed granted by the node provides a significantly smoother experience, as there is less time spent waiting for the totems to appear and cast.
  • Ritual of Awakening provides a massive boost of damage, Mana recovery and Life regeneration. The more totems you place, the more valuable this node becomes.
  • Arcane Blessing is a huge boost to damage, Cast Speed and Mana recovery. Arcane Surge Effect scales all three of these.
  • Conviction of Power gives permanent Power and Endurance Charges for some added tankiness and critical strike chance. Take this last as you don't swap to critical strikes until later.
  • For your Major Pantheon, use the Brine King to deal with Freezes, Chills and Stuns.
  • For the Minor Pantheon, use Abberath for Ignite and Burning Ground mitigation or Ralakesh for anti-Bleed.

How to Progress

After finishing the campaign, you begin interacting with the Atlas of Worlds. Learn more about how to progress your Atlas check out our Atlas progression guide! Or head over to our Boss section to learn how to defeat the Eldritch Horrors you encounter while exploring the Atlas.

  • Look to solve Intelligence, Dexterity and Strength requirements on gear. If you find yourself short you can spec into Attribute Notables as a temporary solutions.
    • You can use your Faustus to respec if a new skill or gear upgrade leaves you unable to use your skills/items.

Leveling Trees

  • Holy Flame Totem is supplemented by Purifying Flame until you get Ancestral Bond. This prevents YOU from dealing damage, but not your proxies, such as mines and totems. For this reason, swap Purifying Flame for Wave of Conviction supported by Blastchain Mine Support for additional damage and utility after taking Ancestral Bond.
  • There are a number of Minion Damage and Minion Attack Speed nodes in the leveling section. This is for easy access to damage via Spiritual Aid and a significant boost to Shield Charge speed via Spiritual Command. These can be dropped later to spec into Critical Strike nodes.
    • If needed, head to Faustus to respec for a gold cost. This is very cost efficient during the campaign.

Early Game Tree

  • Use the Early Maps Tree for the swap to Critical Strike. It is wise to take some critical nodes before dropping Elemental Overload so you don't lose a bunch of damage at once. At this point, make sure you swap your Support Gems to the Early Maps setup as a large amount of Critical Chance and damage come from your Supports.

Endgame Tree

  • You begin using the Endgame Tree when you have acquired the Large Cluster Jewel and Medium Cluster Jewels. This tree removes the Minion Damage and Attack Speed wheels.

Resistances

You can get Resistances wherever you want and they do not have to be on specific items. Resistances are incredibly important though. DO NOT forget about them when choosing which items to craft or buy! Make sure to check your Resistances in your Character Sheet (C, Defense Tab) whenever you switch your gear around. To learn more about defences in Path of Exile, check out our guide here!

Endgame
Aspirational
  • During the campaign, try to keep your Elemental Resistances as high as possible.
  • As a caster, your wand dictates most of your damage. Make sure it has either +1 to level of fire skill spells or a high amount of added damage to spells. Added fire damage is preferable, but Cold and Lightning are decent as well!
  • Next to Dialla in the Eternal Lab (left side) in Act3, there is a Crafting Recipe for added Fire Damage to Spells. Use it on your wand for a crazy damage boost!
  • During the campaign, try to get Life and Resistances on every non-weapon item. But make sure to prioritize gem links on items as well
  • Equip a Sapphire Ring for Merveil.
  • Vendoring an Iron Ring with a red, green or blue gem gives you a normal rarity Ruby Ring, Topaz Ring or Sapphire Ring respectively.
  • Use an Amber Amulet to ensure you have enough Strength to equip gems and items.
  • You can use the crafting bench to craft any extra Resistances for 1 Orb of Transmutation, this works on normal rarity items as well.
  • If you need extra Strength or Dexterity, you can use the crafting bench for 1 Orb of Augmentation to add what you need.
  • Equip Two-Stone Rings after level 20, and get Fire, Cold, and Lightning Resistances as close to 75+ as possible.

  • As you enter the Atlas of Worlds and begin mapping, your first goal is to cap your Resistances and meet Attribute requirements. Upgrade your Flasks and make sure they are the relevant types shown on the right side.
  • Life and Life Regeneration are high priority and synergize with your Ascendancies.
  • + to Level of Fire/Physical/Spell Skill Gems is a high priority stat on Amulets and Wands.
  • Get Increased Chance to Block on your shield for a big boost to defence.
  • Depending on cost of Prismatic Oil, it is important to enchant your amulet with Wood, Stone and Spell as early as possible.
    • This is a massive boost to damage and survivability!

  • Your first priority when entering T16 maps should be Anathema. This allows you to get up to 5 curses in your build. This is a massive boost to damage.
  • After this, target a Cloak of Flame for some powerful Physical damage mitigation. When available, buy one with +2 levels of Socketed AoE/Projectile Skill Gems for a boost to damage.
  • Use a Replica Dragonfang's Flight with +3 Levels to Holy Flame Totem until you can afford Ashes of the Stars. The Ashes of the Stars Amulet is a powerful item that boosts damage and quality of life. Every Skill Gem receives the quality boost. Holy Flame Totem gets a huge boost to Projectile Speed, Searing Bond gets additional totems and Shield Charge gets more Movement Speed. Don't forget to enchant your amulet with Wood, Stone and Spell.
  • Fill empty sockets with Rare Jewels that address any missing resistances or attributes. These jewels offer both damage increases while solving problems with your current gear.
  • Your Gloves, Boots and Body Armour can all have 2 Eldritch Implicits each. These Implicits can be added by applying Searing Exarch and Eater of Worlds currency.

Eldritch Currency can be found from Altar Rewards in Maps after choosing either the Searing Exarch or Eater of Worlds Beacon on the Map Device. They have tiers, for example, the lowest tier currencies are: Lesser Eldritch Ichor and Lesser Eldritch Ember. If you don't like an outcome, just roll over it with another Eldritch Currency. Don't go overboard on rerolling, you have to redo the Implicits later anyways.

  • Once you have farmed a significant amount of currency, its time to double your damage. The Nimis Ring is extraordinarily expensive, but worth every penny. With enough projectiles, each totem covers the entire screen and all the projectiles returns, with one hitting a second time. This is a massive boost to clear speed and single target damage. If you feel like you don't have enough projectiles for this, add a Rain of Splinters. This Ring is not at all mandatory to do most content in the game, but is there for when you want to push your build to the next level.
  • Unique jewels are very strong in Path of Exile. There are a number that can help this build. First, get a Lethal Pride that has useful mods such as Double Damage and Increased Armour. Check out our guide to find a good jewel.
  • Unnatural Instinct in the top middle slot near Hex Master gives a huge boost to damage, mana reservation and survivability.
  • A Watcher's Eye can give strong bonuses alongside Determination
    • % Chance to Block Attack Damage while affected by Determination
    • + to Armour while affected by Determination
    • You Take 50% Reduced Extra Damage From Critical Strikes while affected by Determination
  • The Bottled Faith Flask synergizes perfectly with this setup. Huge Consecrated Ground that provides additional damage and Critical Strike Chance. It is a no-brainer item, but can be expensive.

FAQ

  • Help Alira.

  • Make sure you are always at the totem cap. In the endgame, you can have 4 Holy Flame Totems and some additional number of Searing Bond totems, depending on gem quality. Always know what the cap is so it is easy for you to recognize when you are accidentally removing your Holy Flame Totems (see the icon in the top left) and placing too many Searing Bond totems. Keep in mind, the Searing Bond totems do almost no damage, they are just there to amplify the damage of Holy Flame!

    When in the endgame and scaling Critical Strikes, make sure you have unspecced Elemental Overload as it makes your crits deal no extra damage.

  • There are many good mods to get on a Lethal Pride Timeless Jewel. Roughly in order of importance:

    • 5% chance to deal Double Damage
    • 4% increased maximum Life
    • 1% of Life Regenerated per second
    • You take 10% reduced Extra Damage from Critical Strikes
    • 20% increased Armour
    • 20% increased Physical Damage
    • 20% increased Totem Damage
    • Gain 5% of Physical Damage as Extra Fire Damage
    • +20 to Strength

  • Copy and Paste everything RED:   "!gen|s rec"  into the search bar of your Stash tab after rolling all of your Maps for your session. This highlights ALL maps that can be run. Press Space + Shift while using Awakened Trade Macro to Save your Regexes and name them for future use and use poe.re to create your own Regexes. Otherwise, avoid the following modifiers:

    • Players cannot Regenerate Life, Mana or Energy Shield
    • Players have 60% less Recovery Rate of Life and Energy Shield

  • Remember to not level Clarity too high or else you do not have a large enough Mana pool. Keep in mind that the more totems you have out, the more Mana you recover! Take Deep Thoughts early if needed and grab the mastery for 5 Mana Regeneration.

Summary

3.28 - Mirage
Holy Flame Totem Hierophant League Starter
  • The Holy Flame Totem Hierophant League Starter is an all around build that gets a healthy amount of clear speed, single target damage and survivability.
  • Holy Flame Totem fires projectiles that pierce all targets, resulting in screen wide clear once you level the skill up and give it quality.
  • The Hierophant Ascendancy notable, Ritual of Awakening, provides massive damage and recovery for both Life and Mana, but remember to keep up all your totems!
  • Early itemization focuses on scaling of Life, Block and Life recovery.
  • Scaling Arcane Surge effect provides massive recovery but also damage due to Arcane Blessing.
