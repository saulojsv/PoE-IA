Champion support with fortify + curses + spectre auras

Stats:
https://imgur.com/a/GL9kTYz

I had so much fun supporting carries with my champion and wanted to share my build with the community. I welcome advice and criticism. Thanks for taking a look. I will update this post regularly.

Perfect ritual corpses I use (modifiable based on your carry):
-Adherent of zarokh (anger)
-Frozen cannibal (purity of ice)
-Fiery cannibal (purity of fire)
-Eldritch eye (purity of lightning)
-Forest tiger(haste, frenzy charges)
-Forest warrior (onslaught)
-Guardian turtle (determination)
-Hydra (hatred)
-Judgemental spirit (discipline)
-Naval officer (precision, reduced cold damage taken)
-Pain artist (zealotry)
-Primal demiurge (clarity, sap)
-Primal thunderbird (grace)
-Sanguimancer demon (increased spell damage, heals allies)
-Spirit of fortune (wrath, lucky lightning damage)
-Warlord (vitality, endurance charges)
-Spectral leader (action speed)

Dark seer:

Focus on ES, base can be Sacred or Twilight:

Focus on ES, ignore chaos resist since CI:

Focus on +1 max spectre and higher spectre max life:

Focus on max fortification:

Endurance version:

Decent corrupt implicit:

Replace Bonemeld if you want more fortification (44 or even 47 if volatile vaaled):

Focus on ES, max minion life, and minion resist:

Replace Kalandra & modify tree if you want 2 more curses:

Mageblood:


Elegant hubris above witch's starting area for 240% increased minion life.
How to search for timeless jewels by Fubgun:
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_kYy-s_B-ng

Runegrafts:
-Runegraft of the bound (+2 max fortification)
-Runegraft of the fortress (inc. ES, armour and evasion)

Tattoos:
-Valako shieldbearer (% block attack)
-Hinekora warrior (% ES)
-Ramako fleetfoot (% movement)

Tree:
https://poe.ninja/poe1/pob/91eec

Masteries:
Block: +1% chance to block spell damage per 5% chance to block attack damage
Armour & ES: recover 5% of ES over 1 second when you take physical damage from enemy hit
Armour & ES: increases and reductions to armour also apply to ES recharge rate at 20% of their value
Shield: +1% chance to block attack damage per 5% chance to block on equipped shield
ES: 50% of your ES is added to your stun threshold
ES: 100% increased ES from equipped helmet
