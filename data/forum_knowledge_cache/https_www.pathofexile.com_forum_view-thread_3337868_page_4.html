Cool! Glad to hear you'll be playing this.

Yeah, you can get by with Hatchet Master, if you don't mind not having perma Onslaught, but having it be perma just feels a lot better.

So, I still recommend a double-corrupted Thrillsteel overall, but...

If you want the absolute highest DPS helm, it would probably be Abyssus, which makes you squishier.

Another good option would be Devoto's for the Attack Speed, Movement Speed and Chaos Res, which would be very nice if you're just focusing on mapping.

Taking a look at PoB, I would also recommend giving the new helmet "Blood Prince" a try; too bad it has that reduced max life though...

If you wanna go Spell Suppression that should be fine too; maybe take a look at "Elevore" that gives you life recovery when you Suppress Spell Dmg.
