So, can I ask how you are overcoming the limitations on general's cry? Specifically, my experience with this is that general's cry seems to cap the number of ongoing aftershock instances that can exist, to the number of mirages it currently is limited to. So, if the gem currently says 5 mirages, that is the cap on the number of queued aftershocks that can be 'cooking' on the ground.

If I have a set of 5 aftershocks cooking, and I summon 5 new mirage guys, the older aftershocks on the ground will still cook, but they won't pop when their time is up, they just fizzle instead. Then that second set of mirages' aftershocks will pop after their duration is up, just fine, but definitely not that first set that got overwritten.

Based on the opening post you really haven't experienced this problem though, so I am not sure why I am. Im currently just using a 4link of inc duration + gen cry + eq of amplification + brutality as my test links, with avatar of fire allocated, so I do 0 damage and can test consistently. My gen cry cd is 2s and my eq duration is 6.5s. If I just hold down gen cry, no EQ aftershocks will ever pop, as the older ones always get overwritten.

Edit:
As a follow up, you can even see this overwriting taking place. There is a buff icon top left, for general's cry, that sits at 5, all the way until the aftershocks pop, at which point it disappears. Clearly the game internally has not fully despawned the mirages, they stick around until their full attack is finished, ie: when the aftershock is popped. But, they also still count towards the mirage cap, so using gen cry again while aftershock is cooking forces the game to despawn and replace them, and then the aftershock is also deleted as well.
