Build Overview

This build uses Bino's Kitchen Knife and The Hateful Accuser to leverage spreading poison damage scaled by Low Tolerance and Viper Strike of the Mamba.

It clears off-screen, melts uber bosses, and can run every map modifier in the game for "Risk Scarab" farming.

For league starting in trade league, I would recommend playing my Poisonous Concoction Build until you have acquired enough wealth to buy:

- Bino's Kitchen Knife

- Essentia Sanguis

- AT LEAST 3x Low Tolerance jewels

- Voices (Adds 5 Small Passive Skills)

Please do not play my builds if you are a newer player.

Strengths and Weaknesses

  • insane clear (like 2 screens away)
  • good single target
  • fast
  • semi-complicated
  • not ssf-friendly

How it Plays

To clear maps, just 1-button Viper Strike of the Mamba.

For bossing, cast Penance Mark followed by Despair.

Then, cast Desecrate twice on top of the boss to allow for your General's Cry to automatically apply wither.

From there on out, just spam Viper Strike of the Mamba and recast Desecrate if the boss moves.
