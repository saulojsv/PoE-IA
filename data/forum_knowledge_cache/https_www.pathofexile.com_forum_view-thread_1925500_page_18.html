ShirtlessMan wrote:
When is it worth changing to 6L chest and a normal head?
Is any chest with high life and res on + a helm with high life and res better than BoR?


Yes!

1st have all your gear upgraded: all res capped, at least 4,000+ life, at least 300 phys DPS rare claw. If your other stuff isn't up to par, you benefit more keeping the BoR. I'm at 99/126 atlas and still rocking the BoR on my initial 250c investment. All my currency since then has gone to getting a 6 socket Belly and trying to link it. Just remember, we only use BoR because it's a cheap 6L equivalent. So it's worth to swap as soon as you can get a 6L that combined with a rare helm, will give you better defenses than the BoR alone. All we get from the BoR is 120-160 life.. The Ar/Ev, block, Endurance Charges is all negligible to me.

If you can get that same life off your chest + helm, I'd def go for it. Esp since you would prob have more armor for phys mitigation, more res to allow you to deal with ele weakness, allow you to use more dps focused uniques like Carnage Heart or Belt of Deceiver, you would have more links to work with to let you put more DPS on your totem, add a Molten Shell to your CWDT, Vaal Lightning Trap with Increased duration for bosses..
