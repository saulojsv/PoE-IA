Frost Blades Slayer League Starter for Path of Exile Mirage 3.28

by FuzzyDuckzy
Mirage 3.28Last Updated: March 5, 2026

Advertisement

This website is supported by ads.

The Frost Blades Slayer is a fast, fun and all around powerful League Starter. It uses Frost Blades with extra strikes to blow up huge packs of enemies and freeze all but the toughest enemies with ease. Use Frost Blades from the very start to end game as your main damage dealing skill.

Early on, use Precise Technique combined with a fire mastery to add 100% increased damage to ignited foes, which you achieve through Added Fire Damage Support. Mid acts, you add Hatchet Master for more damage and clear speed.

Level with 2-Handed Swords for the first 2 acts before swapping to 2-Handed Axes. Use swords early, as they have good attack speed and an accuracy implicit. Swap to an axe when you path towards the axe nodes on the passive tree.

Continue using axes through the campaign and early maps until you hit level 78 and can then swap to White Wind and the critical strike variant.

Later on, add in some more powerful and affordable Uniques to clear the atlas including Darkray Vectors & Perseverance before adding in some medium priced uniques The Taming & Heatshiver.

Resistances

You do not need specific resistances on any item, just get the required total resistances amongst all gear combined. You need to cap your Resistances at 75% unless otherwise specified in the guide!

Skills

3.27 Keepers of the Flame
Frost Blades Slayer League Starter
Frost Blades
  • Frost Blades is your main damaging skill. It is a melee skill which fires projectiles when you hit a target. It freezes most enemies and ensures very good clear with the projectiles that get released from the main attack.
  • Before you get +1 strike on the passive tree in act 2, use Ancestral Call Support when clearing zones and swap out for Ruthless Support on Bosses.
  • For Bosses, manually curse them with Punishment or (once you unlock all skill gems) Elemental Weakness. When you switch to White Wind, you can drop the curse, as you automatically mark bosses with Assassin's Mark.
  • In the campaign, use Decoy Totem on bosses to draw away aggro. It makes bossing much easier.

How to Ascend

Learn how to find the Trials of Ascendancy and complete The Lord's Labyrinth to earn your Ascensions with our Labyrinth Guide!

  • Bane of Legends gives a nice early damage boost and makes you immune to Physical Reflect. Do not be tempted to take Brutal Fervour first as you do not have the damage early on to leech life.
  • Headsman gives boosts to both damage and speed when mapping and gives a whopping 20% Culling Strike, which is very useful when taking on Pinnacle Bosses.
  • Brutal Fervour doubles life leech and gives overleech, which ensures that even when not attacking you have a strong source of life recovery if you have attacked an enemy recently. This node adds a huge amount of survivability.
  • Masterful Form gives huge damage and defensive boosts. By endgame, you stack a minimum of 8 frenzy charges, which you keep up with on kill effects and by hitting marked enemies. Endurance charges are also easily gained on kill and by using Enduring Cry. This pairs great with the mastery that doubles charge duration, ensuring you should always have max charges when needed.

How to Progress

After finishing the campaign, you begin interacting with the Atlas of Worlds. Learn more about how to progress your Atlas check out our Atlas progression guide! Or head over to our Boss section to learn how to defeat the Eldritch Horrors you encounter while exploring the Atlas.

  • Look to solve Intelligence and Strength requirements on gear. If you find yourself short, you can spec into Attribute Notables as temporary solutions. You need high Intelligence to run White Wind and high Strength for gear and other skill gems.
    • You can use Faustus to respec if a new skill or gear upgrade leaves you unable to use your skills/items.
  • Campaign Tree - During the campaign, you level using the keystone Precise Technique. To ensure this is always up, check your accuracy regularly and, if accuracy starts to get lower than your life, equip a low level Precision.
  • When you reach the Axe nodes on the tree, ensure you are using a Two-Handed axe to get all the benefits from these nodes.
  • When you are ready to switch to the crit version, you need at least 131 intelligence, so try to find this on jewellery or temporarily use +30 Int nodes.
  • You need to be 100% immune to elemental ailments when equipping The Arkhon's Tools

Gearing

3.28 Mirage
Frost Blades Slayer League Starter
Resistances

You can get Resistances wherever you want and they do not have to be on specific items. Resistances are incredibly important though. DO NOT forget about them when choosing which items to craft or buy! Make sure to check your Resistances in your Character Sheet (C, Defense Tab) whenever you switch your gear around. To learn more about defences in Path of Exile, check out our guide here!

You can also use harvest to swap the elemental resistance on items when you are in endgame

Rare gear in this section is easier to craft if you obtain a Fractured base. This base 'locks' a modifier, so you can use  Essences  and other crafting methods to obtain a good item. You can search for fractured modifiers on trade by inputting "~fractured (name of modifier)" in the modifier search box. If you want to try out how to craft your final items, the Craft of Exile simulator is an amazing tool to plan them without wasting in-game currency.

Pay attention to the eldritch mods (implicits) on rare items as they are all needed to play the build smoothly at end game

Fortify

Use Fortify Support in your 6 link instead of Elemental Damage with Attacks Support until you can get a chest with Fortify on hit Implicit (Eater of Worlds Modifiers). When you have the chest implicit, you can remove the Fortify gem.

Armour

It is very important to itemise your character with as much Armour & Evasion as possible. It is also preferable to have no life rolls on your body armour so you can utilise the 15% Life Mastery. Look for the following bases for your rare items:

Marshall's Brigandine Conquest Lamellar Chimerascale Gauntlets Wyvernscale Gauntlets

Rare Jewellery

It is important to get high attribute rolls to Strength & Intelligence on your Amulet along with Life and an open Prefix to craft 'Damage while leeching' or open suffix to craft elemental resistances. All rare jewellery items should have a high life roll due to the large amount of uniques used in the build.

Amethyst rings can be easily crafted by buying a base with fractured life or resistances and then using reforge Chaos in Harvest.

Lethal Pride Jewel

These are jewels that add strength to all nodes and extra notables to major passives. Use the POB timeless jewel finder feature to search for specific jewels. You want this jewel to be slotted in the jewel socket in the top right of the tree (near the dagger nodes) as these tend to be much cheaper. You want to look for a jewel with:

  • Chance to deal double damage
  • Chance to intimidate on hit

Rare Jewels

Look for jewels with a combination of 2 of the following crit modifiers and life or attack speed

  • Global Critical Strike Multiplier
  • Critical Strike Multiplier with Elemental Skills
  • Critical Strike Multiplier with Cold Skills
  • Critical Strike Multiplier with One-Handed Melee Weapons
  • Melee Critical Strike Multiplier
Campaign
Atlas Progression
4 Voidstones
  • During the campaign, try to get life and Resistances and ensure accuracy total is always above life total.
  • If you find a Prismweave, this is a great source of early damage, but do not spend currency trading for this item.
  • Use a Two-Handed Sword for Acts 1-2, as they generally have better attack speed and an accuracy implicit. Replace this with a Two-Handed Axe when you reach the Axe nodes on the Passive Tree.
  • Upgrade your weapon every couple of Acts, looking for Axes with % physical damage. Elemental damage and attack speed are nice bonuses. This slot is very important for melee builds, as all your damage scales from the base weapon damage.
  • Help Alira for a boost to elemental resistances.
  • Get a 5 link as early as you can either through trade or by gambling with Faustus from Act 7 onwards.
  • Only use gold for an Axe if you really need an upgrade or armour/evasion body armours when you need to upgrade to a 4/5 link.
  • Use Decoy Totem on tough bosses. This draws attention away from you and leads to easy and safe boss kills.
  • Continue using Two-Handed Axe build and passive tree until you hit level 78. This is when you respec and start using White Wind. Take your time with this re-spec as there are a lot of changes. Remember to un-allocate Precise Technique.
    IMPORTANT: Your offhand has to be empty to get the bonuses from Whitewind. Go into options and turn off auto equip items or any identified shields or weapons you pick up will automatically equip.
  • A 5 link setup is enough to clear your atlas and get your first 2 voidstones, but a 6 link makes it much more comfortable.
  • Darkray Vectors & Perseverance are cheap uniques that should now be added to the build.
  • If you have the currency, also buy The Taming & Heatshiver.
  • Equip a Prismatic Tincture with penetrates elemental resistances.
  • You should now have all the uniques listed in the Atlas Progression and a 6 link.
  • The Taming gives elemental damage boosts per ailment on an enemy. You need some form of added fire damage on gear to ignite. Summon Skitterbots applies shock and chill and Frost Blades chill and freeze.
  • Heatshiver gives big damage boosts on chilled and frozen enemies.
  • Ensure gems are 20/20. This is especially important for Assassin's Mark, as this is how you generate Power Charges.
  • Push your Critical Strike Multiplier and Critical Strike Chance as high as possible. Critical Strikes inflict Freeze which increases your damage and survivability.

  • This build excels at mapping and completing content where you need to kill lots of enemies quickly. It is good for most league mechanics, but is designed to be a mapper and is not the strongest bossing character.

  • Endurance charge on kill & 100% increased charge duration is all you need for mapping. When bossing, use Enduring Cry near the boss to generate Endurance Charges.

  • Power Charge on kill for mapping and getting Assassin's Mark to 20% quality generates charges during boss encounters.

  • This build is designed to be a cheap and easy League Starter to obtain your 4 Voidstones and farm currency in your preferred league mechanic. If you want to tackle very juiced content, you need to switch to a claw and shield version with more expensive gear. See gearing and skill trees in the guide for the high budget variant.

  • Do not level Precision to level 20. Depending on your gear and level, you need to run a lower level precision gem. What level is different for each player, but as a rule of thumb you want to have at least 80 unreserved Mana.

  • When you are farming red maps you should find enough Eldritch Ichors to roll Chance to Fortify on Hit on your body armour. Until this point, you need to run Fortify Support in your links instead of Elemental Damage with Attacks Support.
