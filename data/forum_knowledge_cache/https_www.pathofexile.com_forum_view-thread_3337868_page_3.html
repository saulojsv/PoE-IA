AntsInMyAssJohnson wrote:
Hi, im a new player and was wondering if it would be possible to have more details on the gear to get earlier into the game


Hi, welcome to the game!

Actually, a lot of the gear this build calls for is already commonly used early on:

Thrillsteel, The Magnate, Chains of Emancipation, Kaom's Spirit, Carnage Heart, Lioneye's Vision, Overflowing Chalice, Replica Wings are all considered League Starter gear.

Now, being a new player you may struggle a bit to buy them early on, since they are pretty popular at League start, but they will still be common enough that you'll be able to easily afford them just by doing Double-Chaos Recipe:



Then as you progress into endgame, you'll start to swap the cheap versions for double-Corrupted ones, as you can find/afford them.

As far as what you use while leveling, just whatever the game drops basically.

You'll want a nice 2H Axe by the time you start grabbing Axe nodes in the tree, but before that either a 2H Axe or 2H Sword will work just fine! This is an exclusively Physical Damage build, but up until we pickup Brutality, you'll want any type of Damage boost and Attack Speed.

Besides that, you want lots of Red/Green Sockets on Armour/Evasion gear that has good Resistances, and again, with as much Attack Speed and Physical Damage as you can find.

Also, don't struggle too much trying to get good gear while leveling; you're going to replace it all, so just keep moving forward with whatever you can.

Finally, ALWAYS remember to keep your Accuracy Rating higher than Life, so you don't miss out on the damage boost from Precise Technique.

Hope that helps!

GL
