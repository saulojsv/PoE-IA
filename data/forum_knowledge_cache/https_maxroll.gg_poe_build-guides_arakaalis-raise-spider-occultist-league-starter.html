Arakaali’s Raise Spider Occultist League Starter for Path of Exile Mirage 3.28

by Shayd
Mirage 3.28Last Updated: March 30, 2026

Advertisement

This website is supported by ads.

The Arakaali's Raise Spider Occultist is centered around the unique item Arakaali's Fang. The dagger allows you to raise a spectral army of immortal spiders that follow you. In addition to being immune to damage, these spiders have excellent AI. This differentiates them from other minion builds, making Spider Summoner a great choice even if you typically dislike the minion playstyle.

Raise Spiders are summoned when you kill a monster with a corpse nearby. You mostly automate this process with the help of The Writhing Jar alongside Desecrate and Divine Ire. Channelling Divine Ire activates Cast while Channelling Support, which casts Desecrate, spawning corpses. While this is happening, you use The Writhing Jar to spawn worms. Divine Ire kills these worms, resulting in your very own Spider army. Your Spiders have a lengthy base duration of 30 seconds and are also self perpetuating. Profane Bloom pairs incredibly well with the Spiders, allowing for satisfying chain explosions.

You cannot use Arakaali's Fang immediately. You level with Absolution and later swap to Soulwrest while farming up currency to purchase an Arakaali's Fang.

For defenses, you begin with a massive life pool thanks to Kaom's Heart alongside having capped block with the help of Bone Offering. Later on, you swap to Chaos Inoculation and build up an enormous pool of Energy Shield, while maintaining your block. You also prioritize endurance charges and maximum resistances, nearly capping them out on the endgame setup.

You can level build without any Unique items. After the campaign you switch to Soulwrest to farm enough money to switch to Arakaali's Fang and begin playing the build. Soulwrest is very cheap even in the early days of the league. Arakaali's Fang is more expensive, but still reasonable to get after a few days of play. In the endgame, The Dark Monarch and The Squire allow the build to reach its true potential.

Resistances

You do not need specific resistances on any item, just get the required total resistances amongst all gear combined. You need to cap your Resistances at 75% unless otherwise specified in the guide!

  • You receive Absolution en route to Merveil, the last boss in Act 1, from the quest "The Siren's Cadence".
  • Absolution is both your single target and clear for all of the campaign. Remember to take the Minion Offence Mastery for "Minions have 30% Increased Area of Effect" as soon as possible.
  • Desecrate is used to spawn corpses that you consume with Flesh Offering for extra speed on your minions.
    • Keep Desecrate at level 1.
  • Absolution can be mana hungry, so keep leveling yourClarity.
  • Conductivity is used for tanky rares and bosses only.
  • At level 28, grab Raise Spectre and go to "Old Fields' in Act 2. Find a "Carnage Chieftain" and use Raise Spectre on it.
  • Pick up Automation and Convocation in Act 3 Library, for better minion movement.Tempest Shield followed by Arctic Armour are slotted in as you grab more reservation on the tree. Grab an Animate Guardian gem and start leveling it, do not use it just yet.
  • Pick up Summon Lightning Golem in Act 4 for some much needed mana regen as well as attack and cast speed. Link it alongside your Raise Spectrewith Feeding Frenzy Support and Elemental Army Support . The former boosts your minions' damage and speed, the latter their tankiness.
  • Once you are done with the Campaign, your first priority is to purchase a Soulwrest. It is often 1-2 Chaos Orbs even on Day 1 of the league.
  • DO NOT LINK IT
  • Skills granted by items, in this case Summon Phantasm, use all supports socketed in the item regardless of links.
    • Get 6 Sockets and DO NOT waste your time Linking them!
    • You can make do with 4 or 5 sockets if you are low on Jeweller's Orbs.
  • Use Cyclone linked with Cast while Channelling Support, Desecrate, and Bone Offering to automate spawning phantasms as well as maintaining your Offering to cap block.
  • Link Malevolence with Generosity Support to boost its effectiveness on your phantasms. Keep Haste separate, you want the aura for yourself as well.
  • Despair is your new curse for tanky rares as well as bosses.
  • Flesh and Stone provides a layer of mitigation, make sure you set it to Sand Stance.
  • Animate Guardian equipment is explained in the FAQ, he is your source of Temporal Chains.
  • Once you have 6 linked your chest and acquired Leash of Oblation, add Spell Cascade Support and Flesh Offering. You now benefit from both Offerings.
  • Do note, without Spell Cascade Support, you cannot link Flesh Offering to your Cast while Channelling Support, you don't have sufficient corpse generation for both offerings. If you have a 5 link, feel free to add Infused Channelling Support until you get a 6 link for the recommended setup.
  • For more single target damage, you can purchase a second Soulwrest. Use the exact same supports, in the same exact positions, except for Greater Multiple Projectiles Support. Replace it with Predator Support.
  • When you are fighting a boss, you can weapon swap and use Signal Prey. Your Summon Phantasms update their damage dynamically, offering a significant boost to your single target DPS.
  • Replace one of your Carnage Chieftains with a Perfect Forest Warrior. He provides both you and your minions with Onslaught and Culling Strike.
  • A regular, non-Perfect Perfect Forest Warrior also provides the same bonus due to a bug. Feel free to use him instead if he is cheaper.
  • Upon acquiring Arakaali's Fang, socket it with Unbound Ailments Support, Withering Touch Support, and Multistrike Support. You can swap Multistrike Support for Melee Splash Support for better clear, but make sure to swap back to Multistrike Support for any bossing.
  • Despair is linked to Blasphemy Support to enable Profane Bloom. You are able to link Arrogance Support to it for a free boost, thanks to Impresence.
  • Divine Ire combined with Cast while Channelling Support and Desecrate is your new setup for spawning spiders. Channel Divine Ire and use The Writhing Jar. The flask summons two worms, your Divine Ire kills them in the presence of corpses, resulting in you spawning Raise Spiders.
  • Keep Desecrate and Divine Ire at level 1.
  • Tempest Shield is added back to help cap off your spell block chance.
  • Whirling Blades replaces Shield Charge because Raise Spiders alongside Spiritual Command results in very significant attack speed.
  • You want to keep up Flesh Offering for the speed if possible.
  • Rallying Cry and Temporal Chains are only used for tanky rares and bosses. Don't worry about keeping them up all the time.

Pantheon
  • This build starts out as a Necromancer, and later swaps to Occultist with the purchase of Arakaali's Fang.
  • Commander of Darkness helps bolster both your and your minions' resistances, as well as providing some damage. It is later swapped out.
  • Mindless Aggression is taken primarily as it is a prerequisite.
  • Unnatural Strength is taken to provide your Summon Phantasm with Unholy Might. This converts their physical damage to chaos and allows them to apply the powerful Withered debuff. Withered increases your chaos damage significantly.
  • Do not take Unnatural Strength until you have swapped off of Absolution and into Soulwrest. You can temporarily take Plaguebringer if you feel you need the power, but it is recommended to leave the points unspent to save on gold.
  • Mistress of Sacrifice is taken to significantly increase your block chance, nearly capping it single handedly.
  • Once you have upgraded your gear sufficiently, you want to respec from Commander of Darkness to Plaguebringer. This provides a powerful aura-like effect that increases your minions' damage, while reducing incoming damage.
  • Void Beacon grants an aura-like effect around you that reduces chaos resistance. It also effectively removes monster recovery, very useful for fighting on enemy consecrated ground.
  • Withering Presence is taken primarily for the chaos resistance, as you are life based after the initial swap.
  • Profane Bloom provides satisfying explosions to help with clear speed.
  • Unholy Authority allows you to apply an additional curse for extra damage.
  • Once you swap to Chaos Inoculation, you respec Withering Presence for Vile Bastion.
    • Vile Bastion provides an impressive 75% Increased Maximum Energy Shield and stun immunity.
  • In the beginning, you take Soul of Brine King for Freeze immunity and Soul of Garukhan for reduced effect of shock on you.
  • You later swap out Soul of Garukhan for Soul of Ralakesh, once you have acquired Mid Soulwrest Poison + Shock.
  • Stormshroud allows you to swap Brine King for Lunaris, gaining reduced elemental damage taken and additional physical damage reduction.
  • If you are struggling with The Writhing Jar charges on bosses, consider swapping Soul of Ralakesh for Soul of Ryslatha.

How to Progress

After finishing the campaign, you begin interacting with the Atlas of Worlds. Learn more about how to progress your Atlas check out our Atlas progression guide! Or head over to our Boss section to learn how to defeat the Eldritch Horrors you encounter while exploring the Atlas.

  • Look to solve Intelligence, Dexterity and Strength requirements on gear. If you find yourself short you can spec into Attribute Notables as a temporary solutions.
    • You can use your Faustus to respec if a new skill or gear upgrade leaves you unable to use your skills/items.

Campaign Tree

  • Absolution is your primary skill for the campaign. Grab the Minion Offence Mastery as soon as possible for "Minions have 30% Increased Area of Effect". This helps significantly when clearing.
  • Additionally, the Minion Defence Mastery "Minions Leech 1% of Damage as Life" helps your minions stay alive in boss fights.
  • If your minions are still struggling with recovery, feel free to swap "Minions Leech 1% of Damage as Life" with "Minions Recover 5% of Life on Minion Death". You need to add Raise Zombie linked with Summon Phantasm Support for this to work.
  • After that, Spiritual Aid is a priority due to how Absolution functions. It significantly increases your own damage, so you kill monsters while they are affected by the Absolution debuff, smoothing out gameplay.
  • Tireless and Mystic Bulwark help with managing your mana costs without having to overly rely on your mana flask.

Soulwrest Tree

  • Once you have swapped to Soulwrest, you want to swap your Minion Defence Mastery for "Minions Recover 5% of Life on Minion Death". This ensure your utility minions stay alive.
  • Staff Mastery "Recover 2% of Life when you Block Attack Damage while wielding a Staff Recover 2% of Energy Shield when you Block Spell Damage while wielding a Staff" provides a nice boost to your recovery, making you gain some life and energy shield whenever you block.
  • It is important that your helmet, chest, gloves and boots all have armour and energy shield for the Armour and Energy Shield Mastery "100% increased Armour and Energy Shield from Equipped Body Armour if Equipped Helmet, Gloves and Boots all have Armour and Energy Shield".
  • The Protection Mastery "Your Elemental Resistances cannot be lowered by Curses" largely mitigates most of the danger from curses.
  • Mid Soulwrest Blessed Rebirth makes your phantasms immune to damage for four seconds. It also lets you swap your Minion Defence Mastery to "Minions have +8% to all maximum Elemental Resistances".
  • If "Life from Death" and "Blessed Rebirth" together are expensive, then get one with only "Blessed Rebirth" and keep your Minion Defence Mastery specced into "Minions Recover 5% of Life on Minion Death"
  • Mid Enduring Composure provides you with consistent endurance charge generation.
  • You need as many Early Soulwrest Poison as possible to reach 100% chance to poison your minions. Later on, you switch to Mid Soulwrest Poison + Shock for 100% chance to avoid being shocked.
  • You also use Mid Soulwrest Poison + Leech to give your minions leech.
  • Be sure to grab one Mid Soulwrest Poison + Taunt with "Minions Have x% Chance to Taunt on Hit". This provides 10% Less Damage Taken.

Arakaali's Tree

  • Spiritual Aid is important to scale the your Profane Bloom damage.
  • Hearty provides a significant increase in your life regeneration rate.
  • 25% + 2% Attack Speed Clusters are key to scaling damage on this build. Initially, "25% Increased Effect" and "Minions have 2% Increased Attack and Cast Speed" suffice. Later on, you need 35% + 3% Attack Speed.
  • Glorious Vanity with "sacrificed in the name of Ahuana" provides you with an "overleech" effect thanks to Flagellant's Sapphire Flask. The flask makes you leech based off of incoming damage, and the jewel means the leech does not end when you reach full life.
  • The specific numbers on the Glorious Vanity do not have to match the one shown, feel free to use our timeless jewel guide to find one. You can find one that transforms either Expertise or Ancestral Knowledge. Any transformed notable that grants "Increased Maximum Life" or "Additional Physical Damage Reduction" or "+x% Chance to Block Attack/Spell Damage" is great. "Automaton Studies" and "Blood-Quenched Bulwark" are some examples.
  • Enduring Bond is important to scale your Raise Spiders' Duration.
  • Arakaali Life + Attack Block are a big part of scaling your damage. "Minions have x% Increased Attack Speed" and "Minions deal x additional Chaos Damage" are interchangeable with "Minions deal x additional Physical Damage".
  • Testudo is a cheap anoint that helps with block and gives you some instant recovery.
  • Arakaali Mid Flex Slot for Stats/Res is in the passive tree to help you fill out any missing resistances or attributes.
  • Swapping to The Squire requires you to allocate Aggressive Bastion to keep your block capped.
  • 35% + 3% Attack Speed Clusters are a significant jump in damage when you can afford them.

Chaos Inoculation Tree

  • The Red Nightmare andThe Blue Nightmare help with both capping your block and resistances with the help of Tattoo of the Ngamahu Firewalker and Tattoo of the Valako Stormrider.
  • Unnatural Instinct and The Light of Meaning is an efficient package that provides you with an enormous amount of Energy Shield, Resistances, Area of Effect, Regeneration, and Skill Effect Duration.
  • CI Max Light + Block help round out your block and maximum resistances.
  • Stormshroud makes you immune to all elemental ailments.
  • Amanamu's Gaze gives your minions a significant boost in damage.
  • Prodigious + Riot Queller 8p is used to help cap out your block chance.
  • Essence Extraction helps you achieve 100% flask uptime with The Tides of Time.
  • Spiritual Command gives you a faster Whirling Blades than most attack based builds.
  • Tattoo of the Valako Shieldbearer is used on your remaining strength nodes to increase your block chance.
  • Tattoo of the Rongokurai Turtle, Sanctum of Thought, and Armour and Energy Shield Mastery make you immune to extra damage from critical strikes.

Resistances

You can get Resistances wherever you want and they do not have to be on specific items. Resistances are incredibly important though. DO NOT forget about them when choosing which items to craft or buy! Make sure to check your Resistances in your Character Sheet (C, Defense Tab) whenever you switch your gear around. To learn more about defences in Path of Exile, check out our guide here!

Gear in this section is easier to craft if you obtain a Fractured base. This base 'locks' a modifier, so you can use  Essences  and other crafting methods to obtain a good item. You can search for fractured modifiers on trade by inputting "~fractured (name of modifier)" in the modifier search box. If you want to try out how to craft your final items, the Craft of Exile simulator is an amazing tool to plan them without wasting in-game currency.

Item Bases

Early on in progression, you want to focus on Armour and Energy Shield bases to enable the Armour and Energy Shield Mastery. When you swap to Arakaali's Fang and Kaom's Heart, base types are less important. Ideally they are pure Armour, but hybrid Armour Energy Shield and hybrid Armour Evasion both work just fine. In the endgame, you want to only use energy shield bases.

  • Your main priority in the campaign is to cap your elemental resistances, and keep them capped despite the resistance penalty you acquire when beating Act 5.
  • You ideally want life on every piece of gear.
  • The build can struggle with Dexterity. Gloves, rings, and amulet are a good place to get it.
  • Absolution has Minion, Physical, and Lightning tags. This means that +1 to Level of Lightning Skill Gems, +1 to Level of Physical Skill Gems, and +1 to Level of Minion Skill Gems all work. Minion is slightly better, but it is marginal.
  • Bone rings are your biggest source of damage after level scaling. "Minions have increased damage" and "Minions have increased attack and cast speed" are things to look for here.

  • As you enter the Atlas of Worlds and begin mapping, your first goal is to cap your Resistances and meet Attribute requirements. Upgrade your Flasks and make sure they are the relevant types shown on the right side.
  • Your immediate priority after the campaign is to purchase a Soulwrest and socket it, you don't need to link it.
  • Mid Soulwrest are a big source of damage and you want to aim for both minion damage and minion attack and cast speed, the rolls don't have to be perfect.
  • It is important you do not get any life modifiers on your chest piece, it should look similar to Mid Soulwrest.
    • This is because you take the Life Mastery "15% increased maximum Life if there are no Life Modifiers on Equipped Body Armour".
    • Life Modifiers are typically "+x to Maximum Life" and "Regenerate X Life Per Second"
  • Once you 6-link your chest, you can swap your belt to Leash of Oblation to enable both Bone Offering and Flesh Offering.
  • Arakaali's Fang should be your first big purchase as it enables the build.
  • Kaom's Heart provides you with an enormous amount of life.
  • Abyss jewels are a big source of damage, so Darkness Enthroned takes that to the next level.
  • Make sure your Impresence has "Despair has no Reservation if Cast as an Aura" on it, there are other variants you don't want.
  • The Writhing Jar is a key part of the build, don't forget it!
  • Ancient Skull is a very cheap and effective source of damage. Make sure you are not using Raise Spectre and Animate Guardian at this point.
  • The Dark Monarch provides an even bigger damage boost than Ancient Skull. Make sure you get some Intelligence to meet its requirement.
  • The Squire takes the build to the next level, at the cost of some gem sockets.
    • Support gems socketed in The Squire also apply to Arakaali's Fang. This doubles your support gem count on Raise Spiders.
  • The Tides of Time alleviates a lot of issues such as suffix pressure due to resistances. It also helps you solve elemental ailments alongside Stormshroud.
  • Aul's Uprising allows you to reserve Malevolence for free, as well as providing a nice chunk of Energy Shield and Attributes.
  • Your main priority on your Rares is to get as much Energy Shield as possible, such as Arakaali CI.
  • Recombinators and Dense Fossils are a good way to make these items.
