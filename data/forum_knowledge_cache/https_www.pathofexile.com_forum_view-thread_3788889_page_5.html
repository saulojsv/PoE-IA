Warcries are automated except one. You can self cast rallying cry if you feel like it, but it's overkill for everything short of hitting a boss you fully understand the attack pattern of. At the start of the build, I didn't press it much while solving my mana. At the high end of the build, I don't press it because the damage overkills hard anyway. But I do press it vs. bosses sometimes.

You somewhat do have to watch charge expiry. It's completely trivial in mapping since you're placing fully charged banners by consequence of moving around getting valor refunds and passively generating valor anyway. Against bosses, you tend to either kill them fast enough to the point where one banner is enough for a phase, or they teleport/immune phase so much that you'll end up naturally placing them anyway.

Are you on the character visible in your profile with Tabula? If so, that's the problem. Tabula is kind of a trap after the campaign. Without a body armor feeding you armor, you have nothing going on against most mapping damage and will be worn down without overleech jewel (yet), flat out 1 hit, or and so on. Plus, you will have a much larger CWDT Molten Shell once you have an armor-based body armor on.

The build innately does so much damage that you should drop Tabula in favor of a 5L with actual stats. I started off with a 1200 body armor and it was generally fine. Swap out your 2H mastery that gives you hit dmg vs rare/uniques alongside that when you have an armor-based chest ready to use, and you will more or less immediately solve your issue of being obliterated.

When I am mapping, even in T17s, I am 90% of the time just going pack to pack with Defiance Banner and not even using block.
