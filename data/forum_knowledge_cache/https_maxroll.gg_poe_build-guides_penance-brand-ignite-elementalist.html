Penance Brand Ignite Elementalist League Starter for Path of Exile Mirage 3.28

by Goratha
Mirage 3.28Last Updated: April 5, 2026

Advertisement

This website is supported by ads.

The Penance Brand Ignite Elementalist has amazing clearing potential, Shaper of Flames and ignite proliferation combine to cause satisfying explosion chains. Penance Brand, despite being this build's main skill, is cast infrequently in the endgame. It's mostly used to blow up bosses and tanky rares, which it does very well!

Herald stacking, a source of explosions, and ignite proliferation come together to create massive explosion chains. Early on Obliteration is a cheap option for explode, while later on Oriath's End and a Crusader Influence Body Armor are great along with Grace of the Goddess for massive scaling.

Gameplay on this build resembles an autobomber, the playstyle is mostly just Shield Charging into packs of monsters and watching them chain explode, while occasionally using Penance Brand to clean up tanky targets.

Early on progress with Rolling Magma linked to Elemental Proliferation Support and Combustion Support. Before you start using Penance Brand you need Shaper of Flames from your first ascendancy points, this allows Penance Brand which is lightning damage to ignite.

This build benefits greatly from a few Unique items, they are NOT required but you should get them ASAP. Nycta's Lantern is a very strong early game weapon that can carry single target all the way into red maps. Obliteration is the first source of explode you get, this deals chaos damage but still ignites from Shaper of Flames. Lost Unity and Bringer of Ruin are used for Herald stacking, which is a lot of damage scaling and enables the autobomber playstyle. Aegis Aurora is used for max block and Melding of the Flesh, this is the main defensive setup for this character endgame.

Resistances

You do not need specific resistances on any item, just get the required total resistances amongst all gear combined. You need to cap your Resistances at 75% unless otherwise specified in the guide!

Skills

Rolling Magma
Penance Brand
Heralds & Golems
  • From level 1 to level 28 you use Rolling Magma as your clear skill.
  • Rolling Magma works well with Elemental Proliferation Support and Combustion Support, this gives it good clear.
  • Add in Flame Wall along with Holy Flame Totem to give the build some nice single target and a meat shield for bosses to target.
  • Shield Charge linked with Momentum Support and Faster Attacks Support should be used for early mobility along with either Flame Dash or Frostblink, whichever you prefer to use.
  • Once you hit level 16, add in Herald of Ice and Herald of Thunder, this gives you a little bit more damage early on.
  • When you reach level 28 you can swap Rolling Magma for Penance Brand, this uses the exact same gem links, and is used for both clearing and single target. Armageddon Brand can be used here as an alternative until maps if you prefer, though you need a single target ability as well.
  • Penance Brand is a physical/lightning brand ability that builds up "energy" on whatever target it's placed on, when it reaches maximum energy it then explodes for a big burst of daamge, which is perfect for ignite! Penance Brand NEEDS Shaper of Flames in order to ignite.
  • Make sure to keep an eye out for any cast speed on weapons and gear, as this builds up stacks of "energy" faster for Penance Brand, Swiftbrand Support is also used to cause it to explode even faster early on.
  • Herald of Ice and Herald of Thunder should be changed for Herald of Ash and Herald of Purity, these scale Penance Brand much better. Much later on these are added back into the build.
  • Use Wave of Conviction linked with added fire early for exposure. This is removed once you get the Fire Exposure glove implicit.
  • Use Flammability on bosses for extra damage.
  • Flame Surge should be used after a big ignite, this creates a patch of burning ground based on the target's largest ignite.
  • Spellblade Support works really well with Penance Brand IF you have a Nycta's Lantern, otherwise you should use Combustion Support or Burning Damage Support.
  • This build benefits greatly from both Heralds and Golems, because of the Bringer of Ruin and Liege of the Primordial ascendancy nodes.
  • Herald of Ash and Herald of Purity are the most important to run early, once you get Bringer of Ruin and Lost Unity you can add in Herald of Ice and Herald of Thunder. Herald of Agony should only be used if you have the Farewell to Flesh bloodline Ascendancy node.
  • Lost Unity grants the Herald of the Hive ability, this deals pulsing damage in an area around you based on how many Heralds you are running, and is a big part of the chain explosion clear.
  • Summon Lightning Golem adds a ton of cast speed and mana regen, this is the first Golem you should use early on.
  • Summon Chaos Golem adds damage multiplier and chaos resistance.
  • Summon Stone Golem adds global defense and life regeneration rate. There is an option to use the transfigured version Summon Stone Golem of Safeguarding, which takes a portion of melee damage before you, this is a good defensive option against things like Shaper Slam.
  • Summon Flame Golem adds area of effect and increased damage.

Ascendancy

Mirage 3.28
Penance Brand Ignite Elementalist League Starter
  • Take Shaper of Flames from Normal Lab. This allows for all damage to ignite. Penance Brand is a Physical/Lightning Converted gem so this allows for it to ignite, this ascendancy node also grants you 100% chance to ignite, which is absurd value! Obliteration, Heralds, and Oriath's End also benefit greatly from this in the endgame.
  • From Cruel Lab take Liege of the Primordial. Once you get this and Golem Commander, you can start stacking golems.
  • Merciless Lab you take Bringer of Ruin, granting you the reservation efficiency needed to run multiple Heralds. This works especially well with Lost Unity for Herald of the Hive.
  • Last but not least, take Mastermind of Discord from Uber Lab. Mastermind of Discord makes your exposure significantly better and also helps with mana regeneration.
  • Much later on you can run a Forbidden Flesh & Forbidden Flame for one of these nodes in order to take the Lycia bloodline node Farewell to Flesh, further buffing your Heralds.

  • Look to solve Intelligence, Dexterity and Strength requirements on gear. If you find yourself short you can spec into Attribute Notables as a temporary solutions.
    • You can use your Faustus to respec if a new skill or gear upgrade leaves you unable to use your skills/items.
  • In the early campaign, look to grab Elemental Overload as soon as possible, this is a big damage increase. Explosive Impact along with it's mastery "100% increased damage with hits against ignited enemies" adds a ton of early damage. Keep in mind this does not apply to ignite, and is dropped later for the exposure mastery instead. Runesmith along with "Recover 10% of mana when an attached Brand expires" is great for mana sustain once you start using Penance Brand.   • As you start entering maps, look to grab Block nodes such as Sanctuary and Safeguard.
  • Much later on you can start converting Intelligence nodes into Herald Buff effect from Tattoo of the Arohongui Shaman. This is also where Elegant Hubris, specifically Caspiro variation for Supreme Ostentation, comes into play, which ignores attribute requirements, making it perfect for stacking tattoos! Look out for "Frenzy on Hit", "80% Avoid Shock", "80% Increased Armor", or any increase to elemental damage on the notables near it!

Resistances

You can get Resistances wherever you want and they do not have to be on specific items. Resistances are incredibly important though.   • During the campaign, try to get as much life as possible and cap your Resistances.
  • Keep an eye out for any %block shields, this makes you significantly tankier in the campaign.
  • There is a few good leveling uniques to keep an eye out for. Honourhome helps a lot once you start using Penance Brand, gem levels are very important and this grants an additional +2. Make sure you put your Penance Brand inside of the helmet if you use this.
  • Any flat damage to spells early on helps a lot, items such as Berek's Grip are nice.
  • Nycta's Lantern is a very strong unique weapon that grants Battlemage, which essentially gives you flat spell damage based on the weapons attack damage, this works great with Spellblade Support.
  • Lost Unity, is technically a low level item, this may be unobtainable early on in a new league but if you have access to it, it makes leveling a breeze.
  • Lost Unity grants the Herald of the Hive ability, this gives you more damage per Herald affecting you, and it also causes a pulsing damage aura around your character, which becomes stronger the more Heralds you have.
  • As you enter the endgame and begin mapping, your first goal is to cap your resistances and meet Attribute requirements.
  • Nycta's Lantern is still a good single target option, but once you are high enough to use Obliteration, this becomes much higher priority.
  • Obliteration adds a lot of clear by exploding enemies. While this does chaos damage, it ignites with Shaper of Flames ascendancy node.
  • Later on it is very important to have a global source of ignite proliferation. This comes from the glove implicit.
  • Kaom's Binding can be a nice early game option for some extra physical mitigation.
  • Make sure to continue to upgrade your bases as you level up and gain more attributes. Try to get as much Armor/ES as possible.
  • Keep in mind there is a Armor/ES mastery on the tree that grants 100% increased Armor/ES from your body armor, but requires all your gear to have Armor/ES.
  • Cinderswallow Urn is a great addition for mapping, this grants you onslaught, ES on kill, and a ton of damage through malediction.
  • Try to get +levels on your Amulet slot, this adds a significant amount of damage. Replica Dragonfang's Flight with +3 Penance Brand can be a nice alternative. Or a Focused Amulet, but this is much, much more expensive.
  • Make sure to get your block as close to cap as possible through Tempest Shield, Block Nodes, and your shield!
  • By far the best way to scale the damage into the endgame is by crafting a Archdemon Crown. This is a base that comes from the Ritual mechanic.
  • The reason this base is so good is because it adds an additional 2 levels to gems linked to it.
  • To start crafting, make sure it's item level 82+ to start with. Use an Elder's Exalted Orb to make it into an Elder Helmet.
  • Use Essence of Horror until you hit "Gems are supported by Burning Damage".
  • If suffixes are not full, use Suffix Cannot be Changed and Harvest reforge for resistance. Cold/Lightning/Fire/Chaos all work, depending on your needs.
  • Use Suffix Cannot be Changed again, along with a Veiled Chaos Orb or Veiled Exalted Orb until you unveil +2 AoE gems, make sure to block "additional skeleton" if you have an open prefix. Veiled Chaos Orb has a chance to fill prefixes, which you would then have to 50/50 remove. Veiled Exalted Orb is more expensive but safer.
  • The main way to make your character tanky in the endgame is by using Aegis Aurora combined with Melding of the Flesh.
  • These work well together, because you stack a single resistance when using Melding of the Flesh, and Aegis Aurora has +5% to maximum cold resist.
  • Aegis Aurora requires a decent amount of armor on your gear for it to function properly, make sure to get as much Armor/ES on your body as possible!
  • Foulborn Aegis Aurora is even better than the base version, this makes you immune to reflect, greatly increasing the amount of maps you can run.
  • The Balance of Terror with %Physical Damage gained as a random element is another big damage increase, this requires you to self-cast Elemental Weakness.
  • Grace of the Goddess is a BIG single target increase, this weapon requires you to get explode through another source.
  • Oriath's End is a flask that grants a source of explode. This pairs well with a Crusader Influenced Explode Body Armor, though it's a little less defense than a normal rare item.

Summary

Mirage 3.28
Penance Brand Ignite Elementalist League Starter
  • The Penance Brand Brand Ignite Elementalist is an extremely satisfying character to play. This build uses both Heralds and Golems for massive quality of life and damage buffs.
  • Mapping is extremely fast with chain explosions thanks to Shaper of Flames, Ignite Proliferation, Heralds, and Obliteration explosions.
  • Single target damage is comfy because of ignite, and it is very easy to move around dodging mechanics, while still having good damage uptime.
  • Very good scaling endgame through Aegis Aurora, Melding of the Flesh, and Grace of the Goddess.
  • Overall the Penance Brand Brand Ignite Elementalist is quite literally a blast to play!
