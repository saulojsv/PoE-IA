Some thoughts now that I've played with the 500% warcry CDR axe a ton:

The Wild Bear Oshabi ascendancy is great. No Unwavering Stance needed and you can get some evasion. Iron Reflexes and Echoes helmet is still an option if you want to go full armor and deal more damage. Regular bosses just melt regardless and I was able to do 175% quant T17 bosses for the challenge without trouble wearing just a normal helmet. The benefit of a normal helmet is you can get +2 AoE gems for your banners to hit the level 23 breakpoint, reduced mana cost of attacks implicit, phys taken as x implicit, mana reservation if you really need it, or filling your missing stats. All of the Farrul's Will options on Farrul seem great as options for the 4th point as well and are likely gonna be better than Oshabi if a Ritual aura spectre can stay alive with no other investment. I haven't tested it to see.

As long as Admonisher isn't nerfed having 7 shouts autocast feels perfect for mapping. I have the sound off so I don't hear them. Currently using Flesh and Stone sand stance and Precision as my other auras with a Pride Aul's with Sovereignty anointed. You can reserve Precision on life or get accuracy rolls on your gear or just accept that you won't get 40% more damage unless you put down War Banner on single target. I could squeeze in Blood and Sand with Slayer Impact Flesh/Flame for more non-banner-down accuracy but I didn't want to reconfigure. I'm not using Forbidden jewels at the moment but slayer has a handful of good choices.

I was looking for a lifetap Uul's to save a few points on mana costs but after playing around with it I think a momentum corruption might be better. It just feels good.

Most of the graft corruptions are worthless but there's one that is "1% chance to take 2% less area damage from hits per 2% overcapped cold resistance" and that's straight up 20% less area damage from hits taken even with only dread and defiance up as you're zooming. As for grafts you can pick anything really. I'm still using Seize the Flesh/Return to Dust but there's a shock one, a sap one, the Heart of Flame shield. Hard to say what's ideal.

I'm using a Warning Call/Lead by Example medium cluster. Seems perfect for the build. A second medium could be Titanic Swings/Towering Threat I guess. I'm not using a 2nd medium. You can do literally anything you want with the tree as long as you take all the basic points.

In my opinion this build with the warcry CDR axe is by far the best low investment build of the entire league and it's not even close. You could legit build it with only 1c invested in every gear slot. It's insane that there are only 10 people on ladder playing it right now. I think once a streamer tries it out it's going to get really popular. Interested to see what other people's takes are.

Edit: Here is my setup atm if anyone has other ideas. I turned off Fist of War, impale DPS, and Rallying Cry and set it to one spike hit to try to make the DPS fair. I'm not good with PoB so it's not gonna be exactly correct. I'm balanced between tank and offense but you can go far in either direction. The next thing I'd upgrade to would be Stormshroud and chance to avoid being shocked so I can move off Brine King.
