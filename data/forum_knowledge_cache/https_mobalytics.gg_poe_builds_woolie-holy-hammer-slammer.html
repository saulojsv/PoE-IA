Build Overview

THE NEW BEST SLAM IN PoE

Holy Hammers is a new Slam skill that becomes MUCH more powerful when you consume a Power Charge on use.

I will be updating this guide constantly - CHECK MY STREAM FOR LIVE UPDATES / ASK QUESTIONS

SKILL ROTATION

CLEARING

1) Leap Slam & Frostblink to enemy packs
be sure to "animation cancel" Leap Slam by using Frostblink at Leap Slam's apex
*2) Rallying Cry (OPTIONAL - only use on tanky Rare enemies)
3) Holy Hammers

do NOT use your Tincture while clearing - it will annihilate your mana!

SINGLE TARGET

Holy Hammers is able to OVERLAP its damage on a single target - to do this consistently:

!! STAND AWAY FROM THE TARGET !!

your initial hit's AoE will deal damage AND spawn another hammer ("Cascade") to also hit

once you have perma Power Charges, you should also AIM OFF-CENTER of the target - your initial slam will still hit but, now, the "extra" Hammer that spawns (via Power Charge consume) will also hit
this "extra" Hammer has better overlap than a Cascade from your Slam

ENSURE THAT YOU HAVE "Always Attack Without Moving" TOGGLED ON FOR Holy Hammers
Left Click Holy Hammers' icon > toggle the option ON at the top

*1) Static Strike (w Power Charge On Critical Support)
*this is only necessary pre- Ashes of the Stars
2) Rallying Cry
3) pop Tincture (if you have one)
3) Holy Hammers spam

refresh Rallying Cry (and Static Strike) as needed

LEVELING

Holy Hammers requires level 28 and is acquired after killing Gravicius in Act 3

use Ground Slam until level 12 then swap to Sunder (after entering Cavern of Wrath)
you must BUY BOTH GEMS FROM NESSA as a Templar (for whatever reason lol)

at level 28, you can either swap to Holy Hammers (after killing Gravicius) or continue using Sunder
they are very similar at this point

HOWEVER, once you acquire a Romira's Banquet AND reach level 60, DEFINITELY swap over to Holy Hammers
Holy Hammers clear is 3x better when consuming a Power Charge

if you need more details for Sunder leveling, I have an ENTIRELY SEPARATE SUNDER LEVELING GUIDE written for Marauder that should fill in the gaps (USE THIS GUIDE'S PASSIVE TREE, though!)

BANDITS

help Alira or kill all

VARIOUS ENDGAME INFO

MAP MODS

Regex of Map mods to avoid:
"!s rec|er,|f ph|eche|acl|tal d|gen"

  • Both types of Reflect
  • Less Recovery Rate
  • No Leech
  • No Regen
  • Monsters steal Charges

LV1 SUPPORT GEM CORRUPTS (new "Coins")

There are quite a few options for a Level 1 support on Holy Hammers (via the new "Coins") - I suggest doing Coins of Power (red) but Coins of Knowledge (blue) are alright too

best reds are: Endurance Charge on Melee Stun Support, Inspiration Support, Overexertion Support, Melee Physical Damage Support, Pulverise Support, Lifetap Support
best blues are: Physical to Lightning Support, Power Charge On Critical Support, Increased Critical Damage Support

PANTHEON

Brine King (capture all additional via Divine Vessels!) & Garukhan / Gruthkul

CRAFTING YOUR GEAR

once you have 10-15div liquid, I suggest crafting a Staff and Body Armor. details are in the "HYPER Endgame" variant's Equipment section

HYPER ENDGAME STUFF

Forbidden Flesh & Flame Unwavering Crusade is great if you want to focus more on Bossing than "normal juiced" stuff
I don't run them, personally, but they're like ~30% more dmg if you want it

Strengths and Weaknesses

  • great clear
  • great single target
  • lots of regen (Sanctuary x Pious Path)
  • slamma jamma
  • have to overlap hits for max single target (stand AWAY from enemy!)
  • Large Cluster Jewel required for mana leech (Fuel the Fight)
