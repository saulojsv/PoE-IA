yeeah, gamers are quite different and individual. Some want a short starter-guide, some want to go in-depth in poe and improve a skill into full scope.

That is also probably the reason why this guide is one of the most succesfull guides in the guardian-, necro- and pathfinder-ascendancy. And I am quite sure that also champions will like the CI and immortal minion-style.

I would have been happy as poe-beginner, when the guide I followed would have been not glascannon, explained the different ailments and would have had a checklist for defences. Or would have explained in detail how to use and scale multiplying components of damage ... like this guide does.

Some dislike the guide because it's long and complex, at the same time many love it because they did their first level 100, bosses, ubers and 40/40 with this build.

About the gloves: yes, last league they were available for 100 chaos, in this league they are rare. Go to "gear" to "gloves", there the variations like vixen's or in maps asenath's are extreme strong too. Or go to "crafts" and craft on your own. Or go without gloves-implicit and autotrigger the curses with tempestshield - hextouch etc. or against bosses with arcanist brand like guided in the sections "the composition of agony": "curses" as well as "skills": "trigger".

About the Adorned: In the related "jewels" is the "Adorned" explained. It's used in lategame with mageblood. So take in pob the tree and item-tab "mageblood".
Of course, poe is always flexible, you can use the Adorned before getting mageblood. Then take a look into "resistances" to get resis in other possible places and use the Adorned early.

So, you see, the guide explain all these possibilties.
I myself love poe, and hoa, because all the optimizations are so interesting every time.

About the build: The build structure is organized in thematical spoilers like build-infos, gear, jewels, clusters, damage, defences, etc., not into budget.
The budget-alternatives you find next to every gearpiece /jewel /cluster in separated subspoilers, and before CI in "tipps for levelling and gearing". In the list of poe-tradelinks they are another time linked again. In "endgame" "the b.i.s. upgrades" the high-budget-solutions are repeated one more time.

And you are correct too: Yes, as beginner such variations are complex. It's not a typical beginner-build or leaguestart-racer. That was not my goal, like written in "Pro / Contra".
On the other hand short guides only writing "copy my pob" or "get my gear" are really not better when you want to understand poe-stats and mechanics.
And of course beginner/starter-guides often stops at 5-10M damage, reach only one fifth of the ehp, cannot farm t17, simulacrums and ubers or need hundreds more divs for endgame.

Some prefer a straight and basic guide, others want individualizations and flexibility in a build beside and enjoy to learn about poe, balancings and minmaxing.

And stacking auras, clusters and HoA damage is simpel once you understood the skills and have an overview of the build.

Arachnophilia!

HoAg and minions remain unchanged.
Tattoos are not removed.
The Adorned is not much nerfed in our mageblood-set-up. 10% less HoA poisonchances are okay, or fullgeared instead simply rare ghastly jewels are enough for the 35.7M dot hardcap.

Instead the nerfed armour of determination we invest a bit more into the big buffed ward (ward-flask doubled by skin of the lords). Lost armour will be regained again i.e. with rare boots (es-ar-ev) or rare +2 helmet (es-ar). Perhaps the new rare bodyarmours will become interesting.

Ultimatum got loot!
Simulacrums got buffed big, good clusters and voices will probably be more available.


So much new possibilities!

Buffed rares, buffed ward, as well as the new double amulet-anointments looks awesome. Dual-wielding got buffed in many ways and is possible too.

HoA-Scions, Glads and Wardens have some new interesting defensive possibilties.

With the new taste of hate and some +max/cold-resis we will have 15% more elemental ehp before we use the melding of the flesh or we switch to tides of time.

Balancing a few passives into enfeeble as third curse, into mana for frostshield or into buffed enduring charges (Enduring composure on a cluster / Enduring cry + Zealot's Oath) is easy done.

The easy useable kalguur-crafting-bench is hot and also the recombination-craftings will drop new b.i.s.-pieces or will even make skin of the lords replaceable...


The buffs

With cyclone the new valour and banners and the buffed infused channelling will add 15-20% ehp. Ball-lightning of static will be triggered in different ways (cwdt, arcanist brand, new kalguur-crafts or second cwc).
The new Retaliations with debilitations add another nice flat 10% dr / ehp.
Tempestshield shield could be switched to blood and sand (as necro with mistress of sacrifice or with the buffed arcane sanctuary, the buffed spellblock-clusters etc.) for awesome 25% more flat ehp.

Alternative Necro, Trickster and Pathfinder no longer need to travel the old long western path to the Arcane guarding and use new buffed Arcane sanctuary instead to gain 3-5 flexible passives.

The levelling skill poisonous concoction is buffed nice.

With the huge buffed ward, which is doubled by skin of the lords, we solely get each 2 seconds another 10% + buffed enduring charges before mageblood and 20% more ehp with mageblood.

So in total we gain awesome tanky 20-50% flat additional dr / ehp in [3.25].


I will need some days and the updated pob-simulations for all the maths and improvements.
I wish best energy to the pob-team!

Looks like a great league for HoA again!
