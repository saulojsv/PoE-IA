Champion

[3.28] General's Cry Lacerate of Butchering Build | Champion | Mirage | Path of Exile 3.28

Hey guys, here is General's Cry Lacerate of Butchering build. Using Foulborn Uul-Netol's Kiss to lower the cooldown of general's cry.


Build Cost
- High Budget


Imbued Gems:
- Maim on Lacerate of Butchering
- Cast while Channeling on Cyclone
- Lifetap on banner setup

Banners:
- Defiance Banner gives us movement speed and spell suppress, try to keep up this all the time. Thanks to Bannerman node on skill tree banner buffs linger for 3 seconds after leaving the area so this makes it possible to keep up two banner buffs at the same time while mapping. On bosses you can have 3 of them since we are not leaving the banner area.
- Dread Banner gives us block attack damage
- War Banner gives more melee physical damage. (Only use it on bosses)

Impale Chance:
- Gloves (+20%)
- Skill tree (+70%)
- Woespike, Steel Ring (+14%)

Onslaught: Lead By Example (Cluser Notable)

Forbidden Flame/Flesh: "Worty Foe" from Champion.

Leveling: Leveling gems and trees are in the video.

Leveling Uniques:
- Limbsplit (Lvl 13-33) - Reaper's Pursuit (Lvl 33-68)
- Tabula Rasa
- Karui Ward, Daresso's Salute
- Blackheart x2 (Lvl 1-24) - Le Heup of All
- Seven-League Step
- Goldrim
- Lochtonial Caress
- Belt of the Deceiver


PoB: https://pobb.in/pU2j0AhSB1Lw

Build Video: https://youtu.be/nM-fsOGCf7c

Bandits: Kill All


Gear:




Gem Setup:

Lacerate of Butchering - General's Cry - Melee Physical Damage - Pulverise - Brutality - Autoexertion - Maim (Imbued)

Cyclone - Desecrate (Lvl 1) - Infused Channeling - Pulverise - Fortify - Lifetap - Cast while Channeling (Imbued)

Flesh and Stone - Pride - Determination - Enlighten

Frostblink / Berserk / Molten Shell - Automation

Defiance Banner - Dread Banner - War Banner - Empower (Lvl 3) - Lifetap (Imbued)
