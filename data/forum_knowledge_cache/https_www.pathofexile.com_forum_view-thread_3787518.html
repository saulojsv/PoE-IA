[3.26] Sanavi's ⚔️ Cyclone Shockwave Slayer ⚔️ | League Starter | HC/SSF Viable! ✔️

Introduction



Step into the fray with one of Path of Exile’s most kinetic and visceral melee builds — the Cyclone Shockwave Slayer. This is not a build for those who stand still and wait. It’s for those who carve through enemies like a storm given form — relentless, elegant, and devastating. With every spin of Cyclone comes the thundering echo of Shockwave, sending tremors through the battlefield as foes are cleaved and crumpled in a symphony of destruction.

Key Mechanics
________________________________________________________________________
Spoiler

Block Scaling 85-89%

Versatile Combatant is the keystone that equalizes block chances, bringing spell block up to par with attack block. Anointed Enigmatic Defense on our amulet grants additional block and spell block chance when you've blocked recently — perfect synergy for a build that blocks constantly. Whirling Barrier and Steelwood Stance further scales our block chance. Coupled with a Rumi's Concoction, this gets our attack and spell block to 65%.

In endgame investment, dual Forbidden Flame and Forbidden Flesh jewels allow access to the Gladiator’s “More than Skill” notable, which grants lucky block to both attack and spell block, a massive boost that pushes the build to near 90% block cap.
