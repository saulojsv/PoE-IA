Gladiator

[3.26][STD]Pinnacle DOT capped Lacerate Bleed Gladiator

This is a build showcase, mainly because I use a lot of legacy/hard to acquire/expensive/self-crafted gear. However, some of the strategies I employed to scale bleed could be replicated to a lesser extent in league.

Keep in mind: Bleed is one of the hardest damage types to effectively scale. Do not expect to be able to copy this build easily.

POB: https://pobb.in/NvjKC8uJSCpb

Pinnacle DPS:
Spoiler


T17 Boss showcase:
Spoiler
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yHT5bpIvA24


Gear:
Spoiler


Pros:
All pinnacle bosses, memory bosses, and T17 bosses melt. Clear is decent with ascendancy explode as we scale phys %.

Cons:
Not the tankiest build, but not the worst.

Noob mistakes:
Spoiler
1. Most bleed chars use Ryslatha's Coil and Volatility support because it looks good on paper in PoB. These items are BAD for bleed. You will wind up doing zero or very little damage VERY often and your damage will feel completely inconsistent. If you hover over the POB calc chart for bleed and it shows a damage range from 1,000,000 to 40,000,000, your build will feel awful to play.

Mechanisms used to scale Bleed:
Spoiler
1. Chest. Dialla's Malefaction is the single best chest for bleed builds due to the quality scaling. +2 AOE is the best implicit as it scales Lacerate.

2. Echos of Creation. 15% MORE damage per Warcry. Warcries become your new best friend. You will need lots of life regen to counter-act it. This allows you to use Overexertion support, which will wind up being the greatest DPS support for Lacerate with several warcries exerting the attack.

3. Physical damage % tattoos.

4. Medium Clusters. Wound Aggravation and Wasting Affliction. Hard to make, but they scale DPS the most.

5. Of course, a good Axe. Elder Reaver is ideal.

6. Amulet. Replica Dragonfangs with Lacerate or Ashes.

7. Light of Meaning with Phys %.

8. Elegant Hubris (if you can get the right one, 156660).


I can provide more of a showcase upon request.
