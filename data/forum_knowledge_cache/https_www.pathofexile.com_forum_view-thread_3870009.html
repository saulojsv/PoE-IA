I would say that the `Yoke of Suffering` and `Pyre` is bait.

Focus on increased aura effect for pride, get a good watchers eye jewel for physical for way less.

Drop Herald of Purity for now, Unless you can justify it later, go for something defensive in the meantime, `Flesh and Stone` w/ Vitality ideally.

For Molten Shell, currently steel skin would be better for you at your armor.
I personally do: `automation` -> `steelskin` -> `blood rage`

Those frenzy charge nodes at the bottom aren't doing you much good. I would go for the onslaught on kill nodes and change your helmet to legit just a decent rare.

If you still want the `Cast on Damage Taken` defensive, `Immortal Call` is still a good option, just make sure the gem levels align correctly.
