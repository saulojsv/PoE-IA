[PoE 3.26] 🥕 Carry the Game with Your Mercenary! Ultimate Support Banner Build

👋 Hello Exile! I'm Stang, and today I'm excited to share a unique full support build for Path of Exile 3.26 designed to empower your Mercenary and let them carry you through the game.

This build is not for damage dealers — it’s for players who love playing as a true support, enabling massive crits, survivability, and aura scaling for your Merc!


🔥 Core Concept
You deal zero damage, but your Mercenary becomes an unstoppable force with:
💥 100% Critical Strike Chance
🛡️ High damage mitigation from Defiance Banner
🔥 Massive added Fire Damage from Pyroclast Mine aura
📈 Stacking Valour generation for permanent uptime

✨ Gameplay
Spoiler

To start things off, check out the Gameplay Montage to see the build in action. Gameplay in video not speed up this build run so fast, make it clear map fast, same.

https://youtu.be/udC2EvA9TDU


🧩 Main Skills
Spoiler

🔗 Destructive Link
Link to your Merc and transfer your Crit Chance.
With Trypanon (100% crit) or a high-crit wand + Ungil's Harmony, your Merc gets guaranteed crits.
Pair with Marylene’s Fallacy for huge Crit Multi — without the downside!
⚠️ If your Merc dies, you die too.


🏴 Defiance Banner (Valour-powered Aura)
Grants: Move Speed, Spell Suppression, Flat Damage Reduction
Boosted by Aura Effect and Valour spent
Gives Elemental Resistance via Rallying Icon


✅ How to gain Valour without attacking:
Banner Mastery: +5 Valour when Warcry is used
Auto-casting General's Cry
Use Bannerman + Inspirational for sustain
With Perfidy chest: 50% increased Valour gained = full recharge in 3 seconds

💣 Pyroclast Mine of Sabotage
Adds flat fire damage to any hit near the mine
If leveled and scaled via Aura Effect, it adds 4,000–5,000 flat fire damage
Works great for bossing, silently boosting your Merc’s output
