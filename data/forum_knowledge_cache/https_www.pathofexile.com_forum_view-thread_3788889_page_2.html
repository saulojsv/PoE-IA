This isn't the type of build that really benefits from any special leveling. Do whatever you want. Play cyclone and switch eventually. Play the usual sunder and autoexert setup if you want (probably 30 great guides on YouTube I figure since it's common). Beeline toward the warcry and banner nodes if you want to get the build's core identity up as you're leveling!

As a non-speedrunner, I am just going to chip away at the first passive tree as it feels right.

The mechanics that really turn this build on are the warcry = valor and banners linger masteries and valor gain wheel. With taking the 30% refund node as well first on the ascendancy, a rudimentary form of the build that begins to tease how the banners work starts shaping up while in the 30s and it continually refines itself as more mechanical passives/the 2nd ascendancy node is unlocked. I'll be leveling this way because I love committing to a build idea early so I can watch it grow from the ground up - even in builds where it's definitely way worse to commit to a skill before level 80, for example!

One of my hopes of this build is that it doesn't really require jumping through hoops or 5 layers of passive tree/gear Tetris gymnastics to function while leveling.
