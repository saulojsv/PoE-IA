Build Overview

This is Guide for my Ele Shockwave Cyclone Slayer Build for Path of Exile 3.28 Mirage.

Watch me play it or ask questions there - https://www.twitch.tv/ronarray

For campaign, leveling and Early game, use - Normal Physical Cyclone (CLICK LINK)

About the Build:

In 3.28 Screams of the Desiccated and new supports opened a lot of new builds possibilities.

For this Shockwave Cyclone Build we can combine Diamond Shrine Screams of the Desiccated belt with The Winds of Fate weapon, completley negating downside of The Winds of Fate since ALL our hits are going to be Critical automatically.

New Invert the Rules Support + Trinity Support will help with DPS too, boosting out elemental damage significantly.

And Marylene's Fallacy will provide even more Critical Multiplier + Free Culling Strike.

Since damage is already very decent with combo above, we can focus on Survivability instead.

❗As Defense we have: 89% Max res, Ailments Immunity, Life + ES recover on Block, 75% Block and 73% Spell Block, Fortification, 5 Endurance Charges, Energy Shield with Leech and recovery.

All of that makes Ele Shockwave Cyclone Slayer a very comfortable build to play.



Strengths and Weaknesses

  • Tanky
  • Block Cap / 89% Max Res
  • Great AOE
  • Nice Clear with Explosions + Shockwave
  • Ailment Immunity
  • Solid Damage
  • Requires very specific items
  • Not SSF friendly
  • Physical Damage hurts if not Blocked

For this Cyclone Slayer build to function you will need:

Screams of the Desiccated with DIAMOND SHRINE Buff

The Winds of Fate weapon

Marylene's Fallacy Amulet (Corruption can be ignored)

The Red Nightmare for Block

Replica Stampede or The Stampede to move faster.

IMPORTANT:

For Elemental builds and for Ele Cyclone in general big part of DPS comes from Tincture, so get as good Tincture as you can and activate it all the time when you need a dps boost.

What Jewels to use?

Lethal Pride (Akoya Chainbreaker) could be any that provides double damage at least twice.

Watcher Eye should have +Max Res, Rest is not important.

Rest of the jewels should have +2 Max Res to help you cap it to 89% + some damaging stats.

Trade link for Cheap Rares - https://www.pathofexile.com/trade/search/Mirage/eRJqrgJrhL

Why The Writhing Jar?

Jar flasks are good for instant recovery and help with DPS boost via Void Shockwave repeats.

How to generate Frenzy charges?

Poacher's Mark quality provides frenzy charges when you hit marked enemy. You only need 3.

What Stance to Use for Flesh and Stone? (Blood or Sand)

Elemental Cyclone Slayer cant benefit from Blood so use Sand stance all the time for better survivability.

When to use Desecrate?

Use it only on bosses when you don't have other monsters around.

Best Imbued Options?

For Cyclone it should be Energy Leech Support, if you cant get it Elemental Damage with Attacks Support or Endurance Charge on Melee Stun Support can be solid too.

For Ground Slam of Earthshaking going for Trinity Support is recommended.
