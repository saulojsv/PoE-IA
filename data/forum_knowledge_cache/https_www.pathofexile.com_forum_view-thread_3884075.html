Elementalist

Kinetic Fusillade With Svalinn and Mageblood Combo

Kinetic Fusillade Elementalist

High-Clear Speed | Solid Defences | Great for Bossing


Overview

Hello! Welcome to my build guide. I got a lot of inspiration from lolcohol's version of the build and some other builds I found on poe.ninja. The build can be a lot of fun with shot-gunning projectiles at enemies and tornado's auto targeting your projectiles with great defensive layers provided by Svalinn and our ascendancy. I was able to run tier 17 maps with the build with ease and all uber bosses.

This guide is more for advanced players who can easily gather enough resources to pay for the items and understand how to adjust gear.

🔥 PoB

- https://pobb.in/e_UVy8WerioI

Youtube:

-Uber Boss:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Qcbmz_nDijg

-Tier 17 Map:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=RbgvM3FcLw4

🧙‍♀️ Class & Ascendancy

Class: Witch
Ascendancy: Elementalist

⚡ Why Elementalist?

- Shaper of Winter = All damage can chill

- Shaper of flames = Hits always ignite

- Bastion of Elements = No elemental Reflect + Primal aegis for elemental mitigation.

- Liege of the Primordial = Golem buffs, adds survivability.

🧪 Pros & Cons

✅ Pros

- High clear speed

- Very tanky with block and ES recovery with watchers eye.

- Can run most map mods

❌ Cons

-Can be expensive

-There are many problems with the build that need to be solved such as accuracy, mana recovery, strength, and hard to acquire uniques.


⚡ Core Skills

Main Skill:

- Kinetic Fusillade and Tornados.

Movement:

- Dash

- Optional: Lightning Warp since reduced duration is being scaled on this build

Auras and Mana Reservation Skills:

- Wrath (Damage)

- Discipline

- Tempest Shield (High Spell Block)

- Herald of Thunder

- Spell Slinger attached to tornados

Utility:

- 4 Golems - 1x Ice Golem, 1x Lightning Golem, 1x Stone Golem, and 1x Flame Golem

- Sigil of Power (Less Damage Taken and more lightning damage)

- Conductivity (Can be socketed with Svalinn shield but I went with conductivity on ring for better uptime.

🧱 Passive Tree

Keystones to Grab:

- Chaos inoculation

Clusters to Focus On:

- Enduring ward: we use two of these in order to cap our attack block.

- Riot Queller

-Prodigious Defence

- Storm Drinker - 8% Lightning Penetration

-Disorienting display

Jewel:

- Foulborn Witchbane: Get the one with explode mod. This will help with clear. This can be exchanged for light of meaning with ES for bossing.

- Thread of hope: This one is kind of the awkward jewel but need to reach multishot and forces of nature and doom cast or lethe shard if desired.

- Stormshroud.

- Replica reckless defence: This will allow us to cap our block chance.

- Watchers eye: ES gain on hit with discipline and lightning penetration with wrath are a must.

- Impossible escape: You'll need the Magebane version. This allows you to grab reduced skill effect duration. In my testing, I didnt notice not having the two smaller nodes attached to it.

Stats to Grab:

- REDUCED duration

- Mana Reservation Efficiency

- Crit and crit multi

-Power and Frenzy Charges

🛡️ Defensive Layers

- Less Damage Taken (Sigil of Power)

- High Block and Spell Block

- High ES

- ES gain on hit

- Golems for global defence

- Primal aegis will take some of our elemental damage from hits making us very tanky when active.

💎 Gear Overview

Weapon: You'll want a rare kinetic wand for this build. I purchased attack speed fractured base and used essence of woe until i hit tier 2 added lightning. I annuled off extra prefix and crafted prefix cannot be changed and then used veiled chaos to apply attacks penetrate 14% elemental resistances. Then I crafted prefixes cannot be changed and reforge crit until i hit crit multi. After that I bench crafted crit/strength/intelligence mod on the wand and I was done. With enough patience, you could craft a much stronger wand with tier 1 lightning and crit multi. I just got tired of going for perfect and settled. Once youre done you can roll tempering orb until you hit increased lightning mods.

Shield: Svalinn

Helmet: You want high ES with accuracy and resistance. Also, the implicits can have some wiggle room here but I found mana reservation efficiency and reduced mana cost of attacks to be very helpful. Bench craft more damage while flask is active.

Chest: You want high ES and 10% Mana reservation efficiency from essence. For implicits, you'll want endurance charge per second and phys taken as chaos. We only cap our block attack when at maximum endurance charges, so this will help sustain our endurance charges in maps and boss fights.

Gloves:You're gonna want accuracy here and high ES and some resistance. You'll want rage implicit for added damage.

Boots: Resistances, ES, and movement speed and some intelligence for more ES.

Amulet: Warped timepiece for reduced skill effect duration and can apply second curse corrupted implicit.

Rings: Timeclasp for reduced duration and dusk ring for more reduced duration with ES, resistance, and enemies hit with conductivity.

Belt: Mageblood

Flasks:

- Silver Flask- With crit

- Bismuth- Chance to avoid shock

- Diamond Flask- Chance to avoid stun

- Quicksilver- Reduced effect of curse

- Bottle Faith

Grafts:

- Eshgraft- Damage penetration, crit strike chance and crit multi for added damage

- Storming Eshgraft: Increased % lightning damage added lightning damage while standing in radiant ground.

Bandits: Kill All


Final Thoughts:

It's far from min-maxed and there is room to grow and improve. I think the grafts helped the build a lot more than I originally thought they would. I hope you guys have fun with this build and enjoy!

Feel free to ask questions. My character name is rathive or comment here. Have nice day!
