Gladiator

[3.28] CheapBunny's Fresh Meat Summon Reaper of Revenant Gladiator | Catarina | Ancient Skull



Introduction
I have done multiple Reaper build before, but this is my first Revenant Reaper build, it turns out to be great in the end game, able to down Ubers without much issue and farm Simulacrum wave 15 reliably.

Unlike conventional Revenant Reaper, this build uses Fresh Meat and Unleash support, and requires constant resummon Reaper every 10 secs, the playstyle is more SRS summoner like.

Because we go for Gladiator ascendancy, the leveling and transition is more difficult, due to lack of minion nodes near Duelist area and no minion Ascendancy until you get Catarina bloodline. Although I league started the build, I do not recommend doing this as league starter, or if you are an inexperienced minion player. Do make sure you check the leveling section if you decide to league start this.

Once again, Reaper is love <3. Enjoy the build!

Build Thought
Spoiler

Build Idea (Catarnia Bloodline and Ancient Skull)
Spoiler

The build idea started with Catarina Bloodline ascendancy node Prolonged Service. I thought this node is quite interesting since it is introduced in 3.27. And it got buffed in 3.28, becomes 20% more minion damage (from 15%), makes me wanted to play with it even more.


This node has great synergy with Ancient Skull unique helmet.


While this helmet provides huge bonuses to minion through Whisper, it has been very difficult to make use of it, because of the chaos damage over time downside to the minion, causing your minions die very quickly. With Prolonged Service, it not only prevents your minions dying from the chaos dot, the chaos dot also makes your minion goes into low life quickly, and get the 20% more minion damage from Prolonged Service.

(Whispers: Minions which hear the whispers take 20% of their maximum life as chaos damage per second, have 50% increased attack speed and damage, and do not listen to you)

Prolonged Service also allows the build to go all in investing into minion offensive power without taking care of their survivability, which is a huge upside.


Why Fresh Meat, Unleash and Summon Reaper of Revenant
Spoiler

With Ancient Skull and Prolonged Service, minions will die 6 secs after the their hp reduced to 0, and they have to be resummoned again. Ancient Skull helmet also requires minion critically hit to gain the buff, which makes Fresh Meat a great support, it provides massive critical bonus and Adrenaline buff to the minions when they are newly summoned.



Fresh Meat Support's buffs apply to minions based on 10% of their minion duration, up to a maximum of 10 seconds. For permanent minions without a duration, this buff will always last for 10 seconds after they are summoned.

To make full use of Fresh Meat support, we need a permanent minion that is easy to summon, and with no cooldowns. Yes, the answer is Summon Reaper of Revenant.



Because we need to resummon 5 Reaper every time Reaper dies, Unleash support becomes handy.




Why Gladiator?
Spoiler

The build is taking another nodes from Catarina Bloodline, Death Offering.



This nodes will kill any target affected by offering when the duration expired, including player, making the build cannot ultilize bone offering to gain block chance, so I go with Gladiator for easy max block and lucky block.



Build Mechanic
Spoiler

Reaper Consume with Ancient Skull
Spoiler

Consume (Heal): The Reaper will use this ability if it has less than 75% life, there is an allied damageable minion nearby.

The Consume skills kill one allied damageable minion, then recovers 50% of the Reaper's life and applies a buff to the Reaper for a base duration of 6 seconds.
40% increased attack speed
40% increased movement speed
30% increased Area of Effect
10% increased character size

Ancient Skull helmet chaos dot will reduce Reaper's life very quickly and hence triggering the consume to buff Reaper offensively.

In this build, we feed Reaper with Spectres and Skeletons. We are using Dark Marionette spectre that will revive automatically 4secs after being consumed. https://www.poewiki.net/wiki/Dark_Marionette


Arcanist Brand Setup (Offerings + Skeletons)
Spoiler

As we need to keep up offerings buff all the time to prevent Death Offering killing Reapers, and to summon enough skeletons for Reaper's consumption, Arcanist Brand setup is used to semi-automate the whole process.



For bossing, you will need to use Desecrate periodically for offering skills to consume.



Alternatively, you can anoint Hollow Effigy passive to press one less button.


Defenses used in this build
Spoiler

1. 75%/77% block chance

2. Lucky block (Gladiator ascendancy)

3. Decent Ehp (6k life + es)

4. 5% life recover on block (shield mod)

5. 75% chaos res

6. Automation + Steelskin

7. Shock Immunity (Tempest Shield)

Leveling
Spoiler

The leveling process is abit tricky for this build, because we started as a duelist, especially when you do it as a league starter.

During act 1-5, level using Ground Slam alone. Once picked up regular Summon Reaper in act 6 (from Lily), I start leveling as Ground Slam + regular Summon Reaper hybrid. This is my level 70 setup:

Level 70 (Champion : Ground Slam + regular Reaper)
https://pobb.in/iczARfMRaEyg

Once you get enough minion nodes on the skill tree and minion gears, swap into
pure Summon Reaper of Revenant. This is my level 85 setup:

Level 85 (Champion : Summon Reaper of Revenant (without Fresh Meat))
https://pobb.in/9lDjKX1BIcLQ

After you get enough damage on your Reaper (higher Reaper gem level, Replica DragonFang, +2 minion impilicit on body armour, +1/2 on helmet), you may change into Gladiator for defensive bonus). This is my level 90 setup:

Level 90 (Gladiator : Summon Reaper of Revenant (without Fresh Meat))
https://pobb.in/6m67rkrIXpXx

Finally swap into Fresh Meat/Unleash setup once you got your Catarina Bloodline and Ancient Skull. Take +1 seal caster mastery before you get Great Unleash support. This is my 95 level setup :

Level 95 : (Gladiator : Summon Reaper of Revenant (with Fresh Meat))
https://pobb.in/m2dR6VWEFEEI
