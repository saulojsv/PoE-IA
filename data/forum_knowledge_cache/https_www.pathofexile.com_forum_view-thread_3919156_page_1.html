Slayer

[3.28] Void Shockwave Cyclone Build | Slayer | Mirage | Path of Exile 3.28

Hey guys, here is void shockwave cyclone slayer build. Using the new Void Shockwave support gem.


Build Cost
- Medium Budget


Void Shockwave: Unlike regular Shockwave, it does not store cooldown uses; instead, it causes your Shockwave to repeat from up to four enemies hit by the initial Shockwave, though the repeat will deal half the original Shockwave's damage. It also has a smaller radius but a faster cooldown and higher damage effectiveness. It can also be used with axes, as well as maces or staves.

Imbued Gem: I went for Endurance Charge on Melee Stun since I have 5 endurance charges and it's 20% more damage. You can go for maim, cruelty, increased critical damage or maybe fortify.

Mana: "Socketed Attacks have # to Total Mana Cost" mod on body armour solves the mana problem.

Stun Immunity: "Ignore Stuns while using Socketed Attack Skills" mod on body armour gives us stun immunity while using cyclone.

Fortify: Overlord (Cluster notable)

Impale Chance:
- Cane of Kulemak (+39%)
- Skill tree (+65%)

Forbidden Flame/Flesh: "Master of Metal" from Champion.

Lethal Pride: Using it for extra damage mods. Look for these mods:
- 5% chance to deal Double Damage
- 20% increased Melee Damage
- +15% to Melee Critical Strike Multiplier
- 30% increased Melee Critical Strike Chance

Leveling: Leveling gems and trees are in the video.

Leveling Uniques:
- Limbsplit (Lvl 13-33) - Reaper's Pursuit (Lvl 33-68)
- Tabula Rasa
- Karui Ward, Daresso's Salute
- Praxis
- Blackheart x2 (Lvl 1-24) - Le Heup of All
- Seven-League Step
- Goldrim
- Lochtonial Caress
- Belt of the Deceiver


PoB: https://pobb.in/QgU1lYIAm3in

Build Video: https://youtu.be/wXLY2oUfDbE

Bandits: Kill All


Gear:




Gem Setup:

Cyclone - Void Shockwave - Melee Physical Damage - Infused Channeling - Pulverise - Brutality

Blood and Sand - Flesh and Stone - Pride - Determination - Enlighten (Lvl 3) / Berserk

Frostblink / Blood Rage / Precision - Arrogance

Assassin's Mark - Mark on Hit - Lifetap - War Banner

Leap Slam - Faster Attacks / Molten Shell - Automation
