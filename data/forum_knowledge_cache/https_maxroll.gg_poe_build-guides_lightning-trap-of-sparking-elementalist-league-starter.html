Lightning Trap of Sparking Elementalist League Starter for Path of Exile Mirage 3.28

by Velyna
Mirage 3.28Last Updated: March 2, 2026

Advertisement

This website is supported by ads.

The Elementalist harnesses the power of elemental Ailments, Heralds, and Golems to provide a smooth and powerful league starter. Shock enemies with Cold or Chill them with Fire, while gaining damage and defense passively through Golems that self-revive. This build can comfortably go from Campaign to Endgame before taking down Pinnacle Bosses in the Atlas of Worlds.

Lightning Trap of Sparking arms after a duration on the ground, blasting nearby enemies with projectiles that pierce and chain. Untriggered traps detonate 4 seconds after arming. This means damage can be pre-stacked for bosses or thrown offscreen while mapping. Trap throwing speed makes traps become armed faster, and the default 15 trap limit is increasable through support gems or passives.

During the Campaign and Early Mapping, you wear hybrid armour/energy shield rare items to provide Physical Mitigation without requiring too much Strength. Before transitioning into the Low-Life setup for Endgame, ensure you have Shavronne's Wrappings and Presence of Chayula to protect against Chaos damage and Stuns. Additionally, pure Energy Shield rares are required since ES is becoming your life pool. The upside of Low-Life setups is accessing powerful auras such as Zealotry and Wrath by reserving life, plus a 30% more multiplier from Pain Attunement.

Resistances

You do not need specific resistances on any item, just get the required total resistances amongst all gear combined. You need to cap your Resistances at 75% unless otherwise specified in the guide!

Skills

Level 1 - 12
Lightning Trap of Sparking
  • Rolling Magma is your main damage dealer until level 12, supported by Holy Flame Totem and Flame Wall.
  • Rolling Magma should be targeted near the player, allowing it to hit enemies multiple times while bouncing forward. Combustion Support support is available early to support either Rolling Magma or Holy Flame Totem.
  • Flame Wall is a lingering ground AoE which damages enemies and buffs projectiles you fire through it, useful for clearing packs or dropping on bosses. Holy Flame Totem is also lingering damage but in totem form, can be dropped alongside Flame Wall to burst down bosses. The totems also create consecrated ground in a small radius around themselves, providing you life regeneration.
  • Lightning Trap is offered as a quest reward near the end of Act 1. Added Lightning Damage Support and Swift Assembly Support supports can be added immediately. Traps are aimed, then must travel and hit the ground before they can be armed. When armed, they trigger if enemies are near or after 4 seconds, resulting in a nova of projectiles. 15 traps may exist by default, and all armed traps can trigger and hit the same enemy. Use this behaviour to pre-stack damage for bosses or clear tough rare packs faster.
  • Lightning Trap of Sparking is the transfigured version of your skill. Lightning Trap of Sparking, functions similarly, with some key differences. These traps produce fewer projectiles but fire them directly at enemies instead of in a nova.
  • The projectiles have 100% chance to pierce, and can chain from terrain.
  • Fewer projectiles sounds bad, but pierce makes the remaining ones far more effective because they aren’t destroyed after hitting a single enemy.

How to Ascend

Learn how to find the Trials of Ascendancy and complete The Lord's Labyrinth to earn your Ascensions with our Labyrinth Guide!

  • Liege of the Primordial – This node supercharges your Golems, increasing the buffs they already grant and making them self-revive after 4 seconds when killed. It essentially turns Golems into walking buffs you only need to activate once.
  • Shaper of Flames – Causes all damage to always ignite then partially converts ignited enemies Physical damage to Fire. Fire damage is easier to mitigate because you already have 75% Fire resistance.
  • Shaper of Storms – Raises the minimum value of Shock from 10% to 25% while also allowing any damage to contribute to Shock. Shock is a straight damage increase which normally requires critting or otherwise accessing Shock chance.
  • Mastermind of Discord – Causes Exposure you inflict to apply an extra -25% to enemy resistances. This is particularly useful for bosses since they have some resistance by default. Apply Exposure with Wave of Conviction until you have the eldritch glove implicit.
  • Bastion of Elements – In the endgame Shaper of Storms loses value since you do enough damage to apply large Shocks without it. Those points can be moved to Bastion of Elements, which provides passive elemental mitigation based on how many notable (medium-sized) passives are allocated on the tree.

How to Progress

After finishing the campaign, you begin interacting with the Atlas of Worlds. Learn more about how to progress your Atlas check out our Atlas progression guide! Or head over to our Boss section to learn how to defeat the Eldritch Horrors you encounter while exploring the Atlas.

  • Campaign – Lightning Trap requires level 12 and is available near the end of act 1. Lightning Trap of Sparking can be farmed in the Labyrinth or purchased. This gem is a priority because of its superior firing pattern plus built-in pierce and chain. Grab the important trap nodes like Expeditious Munitions and High Explosives, followed by Lightning damage. Don’t forget to summon your golems after clearing the first lab for Liege of the Primordial.
    • You can use your Faustus to respec if a new skill or gear upgrade leaves you unable to use your skills/items.
  • Early Maps – After solving Resistances and Attributes, focus on scaling damage, block chance, and life. Don’t overspend on the midrange Armour/Energy Shield pieces since they are only be worn while farming the gear for the Low Life swap. A cluster jewel with Enduring Composure should be among the first purchases for the Physical Mitigation it provides.
  • Endgame – Gather a Shavronne's Wrappings with 5-6 links, Presence of Chayula, and enough Energy Shield gear to survive without life. Additionally, you must have a Shaper influenced shield with the “Recover #% of Energy Shield when you Block” mod. It is recommended all rare items have some form of Energy Shield modifier such as “+# to maximum Energy Shield” on jewellery or “#% increased Energy Shield” on ES base armour/shields. With these items gathered, switch to the Endgame Low Life tree and enjoy being able to run auras with your entire life pool plus 30% more damage from Pain Attunement.

Resistances

You can get Resistances wherever you want and they do not have to be on specific items. Resistances are incredibly important though. DO NOT forget about them when choosing which items to craft or buy! Make sure to check your Resistances in your Character Sheet (C, Defense Tab) whenever you switch your gear around. To learn more about defences in Path of Exile, check out our guide here!

Gear in this section is easier to craft if you obtain a Fractured base. This base 'locks' a modifier, so you can use  Essences and other crafting methods to obtain a good item. You can search for fractured modifiers on trade by inputting "~fractured (name of modifier)" in the modifier search box. If you want to try out how to craft your final items, the Craft of Exile simulator is an amazing tool to plan them without wasting in-game currency.

Explaining Low Life

Low Life characters use Energy Shield as their Life pool, but do not allocate Chaos Inoculation. This means Arrogance Support can be used to enable additional auras, and you have access to 30% more damage from Pain Attunement. The downsides are not being immune to Chaos damage, which penetrates Energy Shield, and Stuns which are based on Life pool. These downsides are solved by Shavronne's Wrappings and Presence of Chayula, respectively.

This means:

  • You must have Chaos Resistance
  • Your gear needs Life initially, but Energy Shield in the End Game
  • Swapping to Low Life without all the required pieces can feel very bad

Mid Game and Swapping to Low Life

High Energy Shield rare items in Boot, Glove, and Helmet slots are important for End Game, so have them ready.

Mid Game chest is an Armour/Energy Shield rare, replaced by Shavronne's Wrappings for End Game. Your belt and amulet remain flexible through Mid Game and can provide Life, Resistances, or Attributes while other gear is improved.

A Shaper influenced shield with “Recover #% of Energy Shield when you Block” is important for End Game because trap builds cannot leech. Shields also supply Block chance and damage mods such as “#% increased Spell Critical Strike Chance” in the Mid Game.

Early Map Gear
Low Life Swap Gear
  • Buying Goat's Horn wands from Nessa or picking them up from the floor is a very good boost to your early damage due to the flat damage implicit.
  • Equip a Sapphire Ring for Merveil.
  • Using a Jade Amulet can help ensure you meet the attribute requirements for gear.
  • Equip Two-Stone Ring after level 20, and get your Fire, Cold, and Lightning Resistances as close to 75+ as possible.
  • Buy a Lightning Trap gem in Act 6 to bring into Cruel Lab for a chance to get Lightning Trap of Sparking at the Font.
  • Merciless Lab's Font can offer - Transform a non-Transfigured Skill Gem to be a random Transfigured version. Bring a Lightning Trap gem if you are still missing Lightning Trap of Sparking in case this happens.
  • In the later Acts, be sure to have Arrogance Support leveling in the secondary weapon slots.
  • Start looking for Armour/Energy Shield bases when in the later acts.

  • Armour/Energy Shield bases in the helmet, glove, boot, chest, and shield slots provide Physical mitigation before Enduring Composure is acquired. These slots should also have life modifiers plus any required resistances or attributes. Don’t spend big here, focus on gathering the items necessary to swap into Low Life.
  • The weapon can be a Dagger, Sceptre, or Wand. Each can roll the desired mods but requires a different balance of Attributes to equip.

Ensure you have all the following before swapping into the End Game tree and setup

  • All Armour/Energy Shield bases must be changed to pure Energy Shield. Life is no longer important, instead look for “#% increased Energy Shield” and “+# to maximum Energy Shield” type modifiers.
  • Shavronne's Wrappings is required to protect against Chaos damage by preventing it from penetrating your Energy Shield. Ideally, this is 6-linked, but you can get away with 5 links through the Atlas push.
  • ⦁ Presence of Chayula prevents Stuns, which are based on life and would otherwise be quite an issue.
