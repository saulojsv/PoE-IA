Thanks )

Since a large number of players (including myself) probably came Diablo, running a build that relies mostly on unique items does make things a lot easier to get going with.

Uniques are all mostly the same, except for rolls, and the random 2 possible mods they get when corrupted; that alone eliminates an insane amount of info you would need to know to be able to properly assess the quality of, what to buy and for how much, and how to craft, were you to go with a build that uses rare gear.

Not to mention uniques are for the most part cheap, but not only that, but I LOVE being cheap and seeing how far I can get! So, that is one of the main driving forces behind my builds - using neglected/cheap, yet endgame viable items to start and grinding with them until I can afford the cool stuff.

However, as a new player you need to understand that this game is DAUNTING and there are layers upon layers of info you'll need to acquire in order to not feel like a noob...

I had 10k hours on Steam when I switched to the standalone client several years ago, and I still feel like a noob sometimes; so, don't be too hard on yourself when you make a mistake, because trust me, you will make A LOT of them! ;)

For now, just focusing on clearing Atlas, doing some double-chaos recipe to buy a 6L Chest.

3.21 Gear & Skills update!

I'm guys,

So, just an update and how my 3.21 toon is looking. Comfortably farming T16s with this setup.

Here are the latest goodies:



PoB Export: https://pobb.in/DLoROJYywg7Q

-::- Changes -::-

Forbidden Flame/Flesh - Bane of Legends is a nice DPS up, makes us Reflect Immune and are currently cheaper than Headsman. Culling Strike I get from the Support gem, with Anomalous which has a nice DPS bonus to low-life mobs.

Lioneye's Vision - 1 div unlinked, a couple Tainted Jewelers for 6S and around 5 Tainted Fuse to get 6L + quite a few Vaals for colors.

Vengeant Cascade Anointment - Pretty much replaces Nimis, actually better because it doesn't have that annoying randomness. It feels amazing on single target! Definitely anoint this ASAP!!

Dying Sun - 30c, with returning proj it's nice to get those extra projectiles.

Purity of Elements - So, I started with Determination and it felt fine, but Ailments are pretty deadly, so I tested mapping without it and saw it didn't feel much different, so I switched to Purity of Elem. for the Ailment Immunity and since I switched to Lioneye's Vision and lost a bit of resist on my chest. Might end up moving Pride to Divine Blessing and grab Determination again, but for now it feels fine without it.

Megalomaniac: So, these are always fun! I found the two I linked for less than 30c each. The one with Chaos Res caps me without Amethyst Flaks at 76%, which feels pretty nice!

That's all for now..

GL

Ye, the STD prices are pretty crazy, though the +1 to socketed gems is way more impactful than the +2 projectile (you double dip with Empower and the dmg bonuse you get from the +2 on vicious projectiles really diminished going +1% more for each additional lvl), at least according to PoB the difference in damage is insignificant. Even now without the armor I'm still melting everything in 1 shot in T16 juiced maps (heck I can just stand and watch the reflections melt everything) and boss hp drops so fast that in a few seconds the phase is triggered (where it applies). Having returning projectiles is worth it when your hit dps is lower but at 230m DPS with hit DPS of 30m it probably isn't really noticeable (though I'm gonna try vengeant cascade to see).
