3.23 Expensive Gear Update: Stage #4

Hi Fellas,

The weekend is almost upon us again, and I some new goodies to show off ;)

Here's the setup I'm running pretty much all the time:

Unavailable


As you can see some pretty expensive goodies:

1) Axe: This is a good example of what to look for. A 1k+ PDPS Despot Axe. To get one, search for a Despot axe with at least 1k PDPS, within your budget limit. To find the best one, use the "copy item" button (bottom left of the item), and then paste it onto your imported character in PoB. Cost: 50d

2) Double-Corrupted Lioneye's: Not as good as the +2 Proj/+2 AoE, but more than good enough. Cost: 12d (6s/l myself).

3) Headhunter: Sold a "That Which Was Taken" jewel for 200d, so I bought HH and the new Lioneye's. HH has always been a lot of fun, but this league with Abyss spires puking out 60+ Rares, it's just amazing. You will love it, if you get one. Cost: 167d

One caveat, is we lose our Rage generation from Emancipation, that means that we drop Arrogance from Precision and add once again self-cast Warlord's Mark.

Yes yes, self-cast yuck... but honestly, it's not a big deal to cast it twice at the start of the map, and then after using Berserk. I picked up the Rage on hit from tree again, so we can sustain 50 np

I've tested this and it's really a minimal inconvenience for the benefit of having reliable Rage with HH.

4) Large/Medium Clusters: This new set is optimized with the best mods we can get. These are the textbook ones to look for. Cost: 1-5d (all mediums self-crafted).

5) Double-Corrupted Tanu Ahi: Switching from Replica Wings means we lose a TON of Crit Strike Chance, not just from the weapon itself, but by no longer being able to pick up Twin Terrors. This is one of the reasons Replica Wings are so good. Cost: 3d

Anyway, this double corruption lets us regain a bit of Crit Chance, though really, we're still pretty low overall, but without going something like Brittle Conflux from The Price of Leaderships amulet, we're not gonna get much higher.

Thankfully that +25% Crit Chance bonus on Tincture really saves the day and brings us to 100% Crit on Full Life mobs, which, combined with Herald of Ash, and HH, is more than enough to blow up whole screens.

6) Corrupted Ralakesh: Just a cheap and useful corruption on boots. There's other mods to look for, but can get expensive. Cost: 100c

7) If I need more single-target DPS, I swap Cruelty > Brutality + Herald of Ash > Herald of Purity.

With this setup you can solo farm T16 Empowered Abyss Spires pretty comfortably!

Now it doesn't mean you can't die... Because HH itself can get you killed, by teleporting you into a Strongbox frost nova or some other explody bs, and also some empowered mobs will just look at u the wrong way and blow you up, but for the most part, you'll feel like a god!

Here's the PoB Export of this setup:

https://pobb.in/Gi6tpTemLyvp

GL

EDIT: forgot to mention. This setup is Sanctum viable and you'll pretty much one-shot Guards. I'd recommend running Ryslatha's + Volatility Support for pure raw single-target DPS.
