Biggest thing I saw when I looked was no movement speed on boots will make your clear so much slower so get movement speed on boots imo.

A lot of your gear is kind of subpar from what it could be in terms of adding dps or defenses but I like that you got good life rolls on lots of your gear.

I would also suggest getting a good farming strat like harvest yellow juice farm if your looking to get wealth. Theres lots of guides on how to farm harvest for profit on the net.

I would say just focus on improving one item at a time starting with what you think is the cheapest to upgrade. Keeping in mind melee cyclone builds can only really do so much on a budget. Possibly making your character being able to farm t10 maps fast might be a better goal for you then to push into t17s or w/e.

lots of melee builds get defenses from endurance charges right now and having fortify helps a lot. Getting a good hp pool is huge in poe so your on the right path with high life items. Also melee cyclone means you will take a lot more damage from monsters then a ranged skill so you will need good defenses somehow but I understand that's hard to work in on a budget on melee build.

Lots of builds use hugely op items to boost their damage or defenses like defiance of destiny amulet or head hunter or mageblood belts. I would possibly try to find an op item to build around and work your way to being able to obtain it.
