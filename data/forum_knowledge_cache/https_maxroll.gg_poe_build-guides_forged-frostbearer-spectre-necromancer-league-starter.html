Forged Frostbearer Spectre Necromancer League Starter for Path of Exile Mirage 3.28

by Helm Breaker
Mirage 3.28Last Updated: July 1, 2026

Advertisement

This website is supported by ads.

The Forged Frostbearer Spectre Necromancer League Starter is a powerful all-rounder Minion build with few buttons to press. The Forged Frostbearers cast Cold mortar projectiles that scale very well when supported by Greater Volley Support, since the projectiles overlap (an effect known as shotgunning). This interaction makes it so that these Spectres deal a lot of single target damage against enemies while also having decent clear speed from the AoE splash damage.

In the campaign and early maps the build uses a variety of different Minions and has a few extra buttons to press. Acquiring a Trigger wand or using the Arcanist Brand skill can mitigate this issue.

Before you start using Forged Frostbearer Spectres, you need to get through the campaign using Absolution or Summon Raging Spirits. If you prefer to use Summon Raging Spirits, you can instead follow our Poison SRS guide through the campaign and then come back here after.

Early on you play a Life and Energy Shield hybrid build with high attack and spell block chance. Later, you switch over to a pure Energy Shield setup with Chaos Inoculation and 100% Spell Suppression while also keeping the high chance to block.

This build doesn't require any Unique items to function, but the build does benefit from several Uniques such as Bitterdream, The Jinxed Juju, Darkness Enthroned and Unending Hunger. Later on you need to get a The Dark Monarch, Bonemeld and Bitterbind Point +1 Spectre to min-max your endgame damage.

Resistances and Attributes

You do not need specific resistances and attributes on any item, just get the required total resistances and attributes amongst all gear combined. You need to cap your Resistances at 75% unless otherwise specified in the guide!

Note: Having extra elemental resistances over your max cap helps dealing with elemental exposure and weakness.

The Turtle provides a 5% flat physical damage reduction buff and a weaker Determination aura. The Forest warrior provides an onslaught aura and culls enemies on hit.

  • In the Late skill gem setup you need to remove Meat Shield Support from your support Spectres and instead use Congregation Support. With Congregation Support the maximum number of Spectres you can summon increases to 16. 11 of them should be Forged Frostbearers and 5 of them are support Spectres. The new support Spectre is a Perfect Primal Thunderbird which provides you with Grace and a chance to shock enemies for extra damage.

Note: If you cannot afford it, you can instead use Bonechill Support together with a Primal Crushclaw Spectre.

  • You need to get either a level 4 Empower Support or a Cold Penetration Support for your main damage Spectres.

Note: If you use Empower Support you can gem swap it with Annihilation Support for improved clear speed in maps.

  • When you have Plaguebringer Forbidden Flesh and Flame, you need to support Desecrate in your trigger wand with Spell Cascade Support. If this is not done, your Bone Offering destroys all corpses and you wont have the Plaguebringer buff on bosses.
  • Replace Spell Echo Support with Greater Spell Echo Support.

How to Progress

After finishing the campaign, you begin interacting with the Atlas of Worlds. Learn more about how to progress your Atlas check out our Atlas progression guide! Or head over to our Boss section to learn how to defeat the Eldritch Horrors you encounter while exploring the Atlas.

  • Look to solve Intelligence, Dexterity and Strength requirements on gear. If you find yourself short you can spec into Practical Application or Expertise as a temporary solution.
    • You can use your Faustus to respec if a new skill or gear upgrade leaves you unable to use your skills/items.

Campaign Tree

  • Going through the campaign, you play using multiple different Minions which means you want to invest skill points into Minion damage, Minion survivability and your own survivability in the form of Life, resistances and block chance.
  • During the campaign, the most important skill point for Minions survivability is the Minion Defence Mastery "Minions Recover 5% of Life on Minion Death". Combine this with Summon Phantasm Support or Summon Raging Spirit to continually heal your Minions. Sacrifice and Righteous Army also increase Minion Survivability, but the increase is minor compared to the mastery.
  • Spiritual Aid increases your Absolution damage and Spiritual Command is taken to increase the damage of your Minions. Also provide you with extra attack speed when using Shield Charge.
  • Sovereignty and the Mana Mastery "12% increased Mana Reservation Efficiency of Skills" is taken to make it possible to use Purity of Elements, Herald of Purity, Vitality and Clarity.
  • After entering Maps, your main priority is to path to Death Attunement and to get a Small Cluster Jewel with the Enduring Composure notable. When you have this cluster jewel you want to grab Endurance and Stamina as soon as possible to improve your survivability. Two other important jewel to use is one Early taunt with the Taunt modifier and one One with Leech with the Leech modifier.
  • When you start using Forged Frostbearer Raise Spectres, you want to get one Unending Hunger to improve your map clear speed since it grants your Spectres Soul Eater. Make sure you place Unending Hunger in the exact same jewel socket slot as can be seen in the passive tree.
  • You also need to get the Minion Offence Mastery that grants "Minions have 30% increased Area of Effect" to increase the splash size of the Cold explosions from Forged Frostbearers mortar projectiles.

Mid Game Tree

  • You need to get one large Minion damage cluster jewel like this Mid and one Fortress Covenant for increased Minion damage and Minion survivability. Make sure you place Fortress Covenant in the exact same jewel socket slot as can be seen in the passive tree.
  • To increase your own survivability against physical damage you need to get one Watcher's Eye with the modifier "#% Additional Physical Damage Reduction while affected by Determination". There are many other useful modifiers but prioritize this modifier first of all.

Late Game Tree

  • Once you have enough Energy Shield items you switch to using the following Keystones:
    Chaos Inoculation makes you immune to all Chaos damage and removes the need of having Chaos resistance on items.
    Ghost Reaver changes the Life leech from Bone Barrier to now leech Energy Shield instead.
    Zealot's Oath changes your Life regeneration into Energy Shield regeneration instead.
  • To further scale your own survivability and Minion damage you need to get these cluster jewels and uniques:
    Late 1 Small (Provides you with Energy Shield, Resistances and Spell Suppression)
    Late 1 Large (Minion damage, Attributes and Energy Shield)
    Watcher's Eye (Two good modifiers)
    Foulborn The Blue Dream with the modifier Passives granting Lightning Resistance or all Elemental Resistances in Radius also grant increased Maximum Energy Shield at 75% of its value. You combine this with 8 Tattoo of the Valako Stormrider. Make sure you place them in the exact same place as the Late skill tree setup.

Note: The Light of Meaning can be used instead of Foulborn The Blue Dream, but it is usually a lot more expensive.

  • You want to use one Runegraft of the Fortress on your Spell Suppression Mastery by Inveterate.
  • If you have enough elemental resistances from your items you want to replace the Commander of Darkness ascendancy node for the Catarina Bloodline Umbral Army node. This gives you +120-160% Increased Defences with 12-16 Spectres.
  • You need 293 Intelligence to use a Twilight Regalia. Use Tattoo of the Hinekora Warrior to convert any Intelligence beyond that into Energy Shield.

Additional upgrades can be made in the Late ES 2x Clusters & Late Flesh and Flame Jewels skill trees:

  • Late 2 Large (Minion damage, Attributes and Energy Shield)
  • Late 2 Small (Provides you with Energy Shield, Attributes and Spell Suppression)
  • You want the unique jewels called Forbidden Flame and Forbidden Flesh with one of the following modifiers:
    1. Plaguebringer
    2. Mistress of Sacrifice
    3. Bone Barrier
    Buy the cheapest combination.
    If you get a pair for Mistress of Sacrifice or Bone Barrier, then you simply respec the ascendancy into Plaguebringer.

Another very good Flesh and Flame combination is Vile Bastion, but it can be very expensive.

Note: If you have Forbidden Flame and Flesh, you need to imbue Desecrate with Spell Cascade Support or replace Inspiration Support with Spell Cascade Support.

Resistances

You can get Resistances wherever you want and they do not have to be on specific items. Resistances are incredibly important though. DO NOT forget about them when choosing which items to craft or buy! Make sure to check your Resistances in your Character Sheet (C, Defense Tab) whenever you switch your gear around. To learn more about defences in Path of Exile, check out our guide here!

Gear in this section is easier to craft if you obtain a Fractured base. This base 'locks' a modifier, so you can use  Essences  and other crafting methods to obtain a good item. You can search for fractured modifiers on trade by inputting "~fractured (name of modifier)" in the modifier search box. If you want to try out how to craft your final items, the Craft of Exile simulator is an amazing tool to plan them without wasting in-game currency.

Item bases

In the early game, you want to focus on using either Hybrid Armour and Energy Shield or Pure Energy Shield bases.
Armour improves your defensive layer against physical damage while Energy Shield increases the total damage you can take from all sources except Chaos damage.

For the late game you want to use one Hybrid Energy Shield and Evasion Boots or Gloves. The rest needs to be Energy Shield bases to increase the maximum damage you can take.

Late Spell Suppress Swap

In late game, you can implement Spell Suppression into the build which provides your character with an additional 40% spell damage reduction. To get enough suppression you must get the following:

  • Inveterate notable and small nodes.
  • Instinct notable and small nodes.
  • Body Armour with crafted 10% Chance to Suppress Spell Damage.
  • Boots or Gloves with 8% Chance to Suppress Spell Damage roll.
  • Gloves with 7% Chance to Suppress Spell Damage Eater Implicit from Grand Eldritch Ichor.
  • One Spell Suppression small cluster like this Late 1 with 25% effect.

Note: When you swap to the "Late ES 2x Cluster" skill tree you need a pair of gloves with at least 12-14% Chance to Suppress Spell Damage and 7-9% on the implicit.
