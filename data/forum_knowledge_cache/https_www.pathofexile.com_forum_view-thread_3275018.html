"
but what I don't understand is that so many builds and suggestions contradict eachother as far as gear goes, and it leaves my brain shortcircuited.


it's called "preferences". Everyone do things differently, some people are more glass cannon don't mind dying every 3 minutes, while other values defences hated dying. There also variety of different ways to get defences and dps.

but whatever you do, a decent life/ES pool AND CAPPED RESISTS are absolute basic, these are not optional. 5k life for life build, 8k ES for ES build. 75% all resists including chaos. Chaos resist is not optional these days anymore.

ES is sort of a noob trap, this isn't a game where you can roll with ES from the start. You actually need to build specifically into ES later lv70-ish when it is actually possible to acquire decent ES rolled gears. If you cannot acquire good ES gear, stay with Life.

Sort out this basic first, before you go into pumping DPS.

Unique =/= legendary. This is one thing you need to understand first. Unique isn't always good. Rare item with high Life & resists rolls are more important.

Aside from the life/es & resist basic, you also need:
- get all ailment immunities if possible : burn free shock bleed & corrupted blood, all these debuff are dangerous.
- get some armor, use Determination aura : to get physical damage reduction

Focus your setup to ONE DPS SKILL, this isn't MMO where you play a billion dps skill. Jack of all trade = WEAK. Free sockets available should be used to either give yourself more defence, or pump your DPS skill dps. You not doing it right if there are spare sockets.

- defences : CWDT, Flesh & Stone, SteelSkin, Molten Shell, etc
- offences : Bone/Flesh OFfering for minion, aura that benefit your dps skill,

if you got extra mana to spare, reserve them for defensive or offensive aura, don't waste them
