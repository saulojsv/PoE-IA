Your toon looks fine...

You gotta be careful not to run maps that reduce the effect of non-curse auras (Determination), that reduce your Leech recovery per second, and also not pickup any Altars that reduce your physical damage reduction:





You can use this regex for corrupted maps:

"!ra c|eec|f el|o al|'v|acl|rune|gen|wak|net|k at|non|ever|wb" pte

Non-corrupted maps:

"!ra c|eec|f el|o al|'v|acl|rune|gen|wak|net|k at|non|ever|wb"

You can also grab the Warcry Mastery that recovers 15% life when you Warcry.

And your Echoes helmet should be 16% max phys dmg taken for each warcry.

Also, Progenesis is a huge boost to tankyness, which is why it's so expensive.

Hope that helps.
