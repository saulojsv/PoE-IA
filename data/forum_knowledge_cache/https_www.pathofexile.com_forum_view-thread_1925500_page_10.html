1. It may seem useless, but there are many instances where .4s of phys immunity saves you. It's pretty widely regarded as the best and mandatory on builds with low physical damage reduction.

2. I've done both, in beta I took the right side first, and in 3.0 thanks to a comment by Dave I took his advice and tried the left side first. While leveling and doing early maps I definitely noticed Bane and Headsman seemed to be most beneficial early on, and to compensate I just rolled a Bleed Immune flask. Avoiding phys reflect means you have to sell or spend currency to reroll maps you want to run. I felt like the time and currency saved was worth.

3. Winterheart looks like a great option. Freeze is about the only thing that scares me, I actually have 2 flask rolled with Freeze/chill immunity. The only thing you lose is having Int and Phys Dmg on your ammy, but 100% chill immune might be worth it. I will def grab one of these and see how it feels.

When it comes down to it, this is just a guide. Make adjustments according to what feels best for you all that matters is that you're having fun and accomplishing what you want with the build.

-I haven't tried, but it should be viable since your Whirl Blade speed is so high, it should be easy to dodge all her stuff.

-Get mid/high life rolls on gear. I currently have 288 life on my gear, and no % life on my jewels and am sitting at 4,700 health.

Lets say you get high tier 2/low tier 1 life on all gear. 70 life on rings + ammy, 80 life on gloves, boots, belt and 5% life on all 4 jewels. That would be 480 life on gear with 20% increased life. I ran these calcs in PoB and it put me about 5,600. This should be really easy and somewhat cheap because you only need life and 2 res on each gear, since we use so many rares it's easy to cap res with each piece of gear only having 2 res. If you farm up 6-10 ex or craft one, a 6 link Belly of the Beast with a 80 life helm will get you about 6,200 life.

Ok, guys, I've played this build to lvl 86, Atziri and uber lab done (0 death and killed uber Argus and Izzaro easily, no problems with uber traps either - very high life regen (>480 atm) helps a lot). Now doing t10+ maps

First.. here is my pastebin link - https://pastebin.com/cHApdXDE . You can copy it and then Path of Building use 'Import/export build' and press the 'Import from Pastebin' button.

You can see my gear and tree. I made some changes compared to the original build by TheNotoriousPIG (thanks to him for his great job!). I really think Blade Barrier, Aspect of the Panther and Weapon Artistry are vital to this build. Besides nice damage bonuses they give block chance and you should not neglect it. Block chance is a good addition to our survival means. Do not forget that BoR has a chance for Endurance Charge on block ) For mana leech i use 1 jewel with 0.2%+ mana leech on it.

Then I took Combat Stamina. Why life regen is important? It adds to our life leech rate due to the Brutal Fervour. And our total life per second (combined from life leech and life regen) becomes 2800+ per second ( I have 2320 life leech rate and 488 life per second)! For that high life regen you need to use Stone Golem. Flame Golem is nice but it is very squishy and its damage boost is not very noticable in high tier maps anyway (20% increased damage at lvl20 it is not more so give just a bit of dps). I think better be more tanky.

For more dps I use Belt of the Deceiver it gives huge 10% increased damage taken by monsters so it works like a multiplier. For reference without it i have 403k total DPS and with it - 441k. It is because it intimidates all enemies in 25 unit radius.

For amulet you should use Carnage Heart. It gives very needed All Attributes + up to 40% increased damage while leeching and we leech 99% of the time. And do not forget it gives us up to 2% life leech too. I don't think you can find a better amulet for this build. It has no life... but still I have 5400 life. Jewels are good too but only with 6%+ life and at least 1 damage bonus. Do not use them without % increased maximum life stat - no point in doing that.

Lion's Roar flask is a musthave too! Huge DPS boost and it is a granite flask so +armour helps as well.

Now about offhand. Singularity! It is cheap and very effective. DO NOT LOOK AT INGAME DPS TOOLTIP!! Use PoB. With Singularity in off-and (and with the 'is the enemy hindered' checked in PoB configurations) you get 80% perma increased damage! And you then only need to concentrate on buying a good main hand claw. For more information - with my main hand claw (317 DPS) + Singularity I have 441k total DPS. While if I would equip two claws with 317 DPS both i get only 402k DPS. You can see that for yourself in the pastebin link i gave above.

About gems in BoR. Blade Flurry - Ruthless - Maim - Chance to Bleed. Technically speakin Added Fire damage is a tiny bit better than Chance to Bleed but you will get less life leech because our main life leech source is physical damage which Added fire damage gem doesn't provide at all. But you can use it if you like. And of course Concentrated Effect is better than any of those support gems but at a price - it makes mapping clunky and replacing it with Inc. Area damage drops our DPS by a huge ammount! So I personally like Ruthless more. And bosses melt in seconds anyway.

The last but not the least - Blood rage gem (with 20% quality!). IT is awesome! Ust it. The damage you gain from it is very huge. To compare with Damage at full life gem I have 432k total DPS while with 3 Frenzy charges from Blood rage I have 441k. And besides Blood Rage leeches! Another reason - we are NOT always at full life. Losing even 10 life drops our DPS significantly. So I really think using Blade Rage is better.

This is my skill tree atm just in case - http://poeurl.com/bslh
