Horken007 wrote:

I want to make an addition to these to clarify. It's a clear speed oriented build that does bosses good enough. When you say Lacerman is not really a boss killing build(which is actually right), some people might understand it as "It's bad at killing bosses". But it's not, as can be seen in your Sirus video:) Other A8 deathless video has 1m lower DPS than current Required Gear which is around 15ex+gems as well. Also boss DPS of Meta Gear is recently increased by up to %27 and had another %5.7 increase today so it's better than last league now. I want to paste this from Cons part of Pros and Cons section to clear things up:
"More oriented to fast clearspeed than bossing (With bossing getting much better at Meta Gear stats)"

Another thing is when we think about time we spend on clearing vs. time spent on endgame bosses, clearing is like %95 of the total time. That's the reason I made the build more oriented to clear speed and keeping it that way. I can sacrifice clear speed and improve bossing, but I want it to stay like this as I think it's more profitting and fun this way. But I also understand people who enjoy melting down endgame bosses real fast more than having fast clear speed. It's all about having fun the way you want in the end:)
