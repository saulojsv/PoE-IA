cheap armor stack champion (perma onslaught)



alright so the basic idea here is to use the champion perma fortify and get perma onslaught from belt and some more effect from chest. with balbala keystone you need one less flask from having onslaught so you get double the flask bonus. with the two flask wheels near the ranger area you get perma flask uptime. put tincture when flasks are filled for bossing. i did sanctums from around level 90 when in low tier red maps. the main idea was to run low lvl sanctums with semi mageblood.



found a +6 helmet for cheap, but you can use either a crafted rare or devotos.



belt flask and tincture. use incr charge recovery for balbala keystone.



rest of the gear is usual armor stack with rares for resist/stats/life.
