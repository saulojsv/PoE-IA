Carrion Golems & Zombies Necromancer League Starter for Path of Exile Mirage 3.28

by Helm Breaker
Mirage 3.28Last Updated: July 1, 2026

Advertisement

This website is supported by ads.

The Carrion Golems & Zombies Necromancer League Starter is a powerful all-rounder Minion build. This build uses Convocation to stack minions, improving clear and allowing you to focus all your damage on a single target.

The build uses Carrion Golems and Zombies to deal high Poison and Chaos damage. Your clear speed relies on items that create explosions such as Asenath's Gentle Touch or Cane of Kulemak equipped to your Animate Guardian.

In the campaign and early maps the build uses a variety of different Minions and has a few extra buttons to press. But later, you fix this by acquiring a Trigger wand.

Before you start using Summon Carrion Golem of Hordes or Summon Carrion Golem of Scavenging you need to get through the campaign using Absolution or Summon Raging Spirits. If you prefer to use Summon Raging Spirits, you can instead follow our Poison SRS guide through the campaign and then come back here after.

For defenses you rely on a high Life pool, Life regen, Life leech, Endurance charges, Physical damage reduction, Energy Shield, increasing Maximum Elemental resistances and high attack and spell block chance.

This build doesn't require any Unique items to function, but the build does benefit from several Uniques such as Bones of Ullr, The Jinxed Juju, Darkness Enthroned and The Covenant. Later on you need to get a Ashes of the Stars, Amanamu's Gaze, Primordial Might, Primordial Harmony, The Anima Stone and Rational Doctrine to min-max your endgame damage.

Resistances and Attributes

You do not need specific resistances and attributes on any item, just get the required total resistances and attributes amongst all gear combined. You need to cap your Resistances at 75% unless otherwise specified in the guide!

Note: Having extra elemental resistances over your max cap helps dealing with elemental exposure and weakness.

  • Use Rolling Magma or Freezing Pulse as your main skill until level 4. As soon as you finish the "Breaking Some Eggs" quest you replace the previous skill with Summon Raging Spirit. Buy Holy Flame Totem and Flame Wall from Nessa for two extra damage skills.
  • You can get Chance to Poison Support by muling it from a Shadow alt after killing Hillock or You can get the gem from the optional "Mercy Mission" quest in Act 1 as a Witch.
  • After killing the last boss of Act 1 (Merveil), replace Summon Raging Spirit with Absolution.
  • Use Flesh Offering on enemy corpses to buff your Minions with attack, cast and movement speed.
  • Replace Holy Flame Totem when you get Herald of Purity.
  • Your Raise Zombies can easily die before you reach level 21 and allocate the Minion Defence Mastery "Minions recover 5% of life on Minion death". Before you have the Mastery don't waste time re-summoning them if they keep dying, but make sure to socket and level your gem in the meantime.
  • Make sure not to level Clarity and Vitality too many times because of the increased mana reservation.
  • In Act 2 "The Vaal Ruins" you can find a corruption altar, use this on your Absolution gem for a chance to turn it into Vaal Absolution. (If you level up or de-level the gem, go back to Act1 and buy a new one)
  • After completing the quest "Lost in Love" in Act 3 take Conductivity to curse rares and bosses for increased damage.
  • When you get Raise Spectre, go back to Act 2 The Old Fields to raise a Carnage Chieftain as a Spectre by casting Desecrate to spawn the corpses from that area. Hold down A and move your mouse over the corpses to be able to target a specific one. After raising the Spectre put Desecrate in your inventory, you do not need the gem anymore for now.
  • You can get many of the gems you need such as Added Chaos Damage Support around level 31 by completeing Siosa's quest in The Library (Act3).
    • Convocation can be found from the last purchase page (7).
  • In Act 4 use Summon Stone Golem for the Life regeneration buff.
  • Around level 46 when you have both Sovereignty and the Mana Mastery "12% increased Mana Reservation Efficiency of Skills", equip and activate Purity of Elements.
  • When possible buy two extra Raise Spectre gems and one Animate Guardian gem. Keep these in your weapon swap to level them up during acts and maps. Use a Vaal Orb on them later when they are lvl 20 for a chance to corrupt them to lvl 21.
  • Despair replaces Conductivity when you finish the third labyrinth for Unnatural Strength.
  • After the Campaign, you can continue to use Absolution or Summon Raging Spirit in white and yellow maps until you have the correct support and skill gems.
    • Make sure your Summon Carrion Golem of Hordes and Raise Zombie skill gems are at least level 15 or higher before swapping.
  • When swapping to Golems and Zombies, replace Herald of Purity and Vitality with Tempest Shield and Flesh and Stone. Make sure you use Sand stance with Flesh and Stone.
  • Replace Summon Stone Golem and Faster Attacks Support with Steelskin linked with Automation.
  • You need to get Void Manipulation Support, Multistrike Support and Melee Splash Support for your Golems and Zombies. For mapping you use Melee Splash Support and then swap it out for Multistrike Support when you fight bosses.
  • When you use a trigger wand for Flesh Offering and Desecrate make sure you keep Desecrate at level 1 to reduce the mana cost.
  • You need to use an Animate Guardian gem and equip it with the suggested items in the FAQ or the AG gear set examples.

Note: If your AG has Asenath's Gentle Touch or Cane of Kulemak and Gravebind you no longer need to use Melee Splash Support.

  • Switch your Spectres to one Carnage Chieftain and two corpse Spectres from the Ritual Mechanic that provide you with buffs and auras. Use a Perfect Guardian Turtle for the 5% flat physical damage reduction buff and a weaker Determination aura. The Perfect Forest Warrior gives you and your minions an onslaught aura and culls enemies on hit. These cannot be shared with other players so you need to find them or buy them from other players. You can look at the FAQ for correct Spectre setups.

Note: Make sure to remove Flesh Offering and Bone Offering from your trigger wand first while raising your corpse Spectres. A Perfect Guardian Turtle can be quite difficult to find or expensive to buy in trade league. You can use an Arena Master for more damage until you acquire the right Spectre corpse.

  • Around the time you reach red tier maps you want to switch to Bone Offering instead of Flesh Offering. Bone Offering massively improves the survivability of your Minions and it provides you with a lot of block chance if you have the Mistress of Sacrifice Ascendancy node.

Note: Respec Commander of Darkness to Mistress of Sacrifice when you have enough elemental resistances (105%) to be overcapped without it.

  • ReplaceAbsolution with Stormblast Mine linked with Minefield Support. This skill is only used to debuff tanky rares and bosses so that they take 3% increased damage per nearby mine. You should never use the detonate mine skill.

Note: Use a level 1 Stormblast Mine, never level up this skill gem. You do however want to have 20 quality on it later.

  • When your Raise Spectre gem reaches level 25 and you have Death Attunement anointed, your maximum Spectre count should be 4. Now you want to use an Arena Master Spectre as the fourth Spectre. It is possible to share this Spectre with other players so you can ask in the Minion global chat /Global 6666 if someone can share them with you.

Note: Remove Bone Offering and Flesh Offering from your trigger wand before traveling to a player's hideout for your Spectres. After this is done you cast Desecrate to spawn corpses in their hideout. (It can take a couple of Desecrate casts before the correct corpse spawns) Hold down "A" while you have the Raise Spectre skill selected to read the name of the corpse you are targeting.

  • When your Minion's Poison chance is 100% without Chance to Poison Support, replace it with Ruthless Support.

Mid Game

  • Replace your Summon Carrion Golem of Hordes with Summon Carrion Golem of Scavenging after anointing Golem Commander on your amulet and socketing The Anima Stone together with 3 primordial jewels.

WARNING: Make sure you remove the Summon Carrion Golem of Scavenging skill gem when you are trying to raise Spectres, since the Scavenging Golems destroy any Spectre corpse on the ground. Always go to a town when adjusting equipment and remove the Golem gem if you end up moving the Raise Spectre gem.

  • Replace your Arena Master with a Perfect Warlord corpse Spectre. Since a Perfect Warlord generates Endurance Charges you can now remove your Small Cluster Jewel.
  • When you have Rational Doctrine and Bitter Heresy, you want to swap from Bone Offering to using Flesh Offering again.

Note: If you have issues with the survivability of your Golems and Zombies after this change, go back to using Bone Offering instead.

Late Game

  • Replace your Carnage Chieftain with the Perfect Forest Tiger corpse Spectre.

Note: This corpse Spectre can be very expensive and can be ignored. Continue using Carnage Chieftain instead. Remove Summon Carrion Golem of Scavenging and your Offering skill gems when you raise this Spectre.

  • After equipping Late and Late, remove Stormblast Mine and Minefield Support. This is to reduce the amount of buttons you need to press, the damage increase the extra jewel socket slots provide are about the same.
  • At this point you are Poison damage capped outside of Uber encounters and Simulacrum without Unbound Ailments Support. This means Melee Physical Damage Support is the better support for mapping and non-uber pinnacle fights.

Note: For late Option 1 & 2 use a level 4 Empower Support instead of Unbound Ailments Support.

  • In the Late Option 1 setup you want to use Bone Offering with Mistress of Sacrifice.
  • In the Late Option 2 setup you want to use Flesh Offering instead.
  • Replace Multistrike Support with Greater Multistrike Support.

On your support Animate Guardian or Raise Spectre gem you want to get one of two options:

  1. Feeding Frenzy Support (preferred)
  2. Summon Phantasm Support (Not recommended)

This opens up the possibility to link the skill gem with the new Congregation Support for an extra Spectre such as:

  1. Carnage Chieftain (If you have a Perfect Tiger then this would improves the uptime for frenzy charges)
  2. Perfect Conjuror of Rot (Malevolence aura)

How to Progress

After finishing the campaign, you begin interacting with the Atlas of Worlds. Learn more about how to progress your Atlas check out our Atlas progression guide! Or head over to our Boss section to learn how to defeat the Eldritch Horrors you encounter while exploring the Atlas.

  • Look to solve Intelligence, Dexterity and Strength requirements on gear. If you find yourself short you can spec into Practical Application or Expertise as a temporary solution.
    • You can use your Faustus to respec if a new skill or gear upgrade leaves you unable to use your skills/items.

Campaign Tree

  • Going through the campaign, you play using multiple different Minions which means you want to invest skill points into Minion damage, Minion survivability and your own survivability in the form of Life, resistances and block chance.
  • During the campaign, the most important skill point for Minions survivability is the Minion Defence Mastery "Minions Recover 5% of Life on Minion Death". Combine this with Summon Phantasm Support or Summon Raging Spirit to continually heal your Minions. Sacrifice and Righteous Army also increase Minion Survivability, but the increase is minor compared to the mastery.
  • Spiritual Aid increases your Absolution damage and Spiritual Command boost your Minion's damage while providing you with extra attack speed for Shield Charge.
  • Sovereignty and the Mana Mastery "12% increased Mana Reservation Efficiency of Skills" makes it possible to use Purity of Elements, Herald of Purity, Vitality and Clarity.

After Campaign & Early Tree

  • After entering Maps, your main priority is to path to Death Attunement and to get a Small Cluster Jewel with the Enduring Composure notable. After this, grab Endurance and Stamina as soon as possible to improve your survivability. Two other important jewels to use are One Abyss Jewel with taunt and One Abyss Jewel with Leech.
  • Another jewel you need to get is a medium cluster jewel like this Life from Death jewel early with the Life from Death notable.

Note: Remember to respec your Minion Defence Mastery node to Minions have +8 to all maximum elemental resistances AFTER you get the medium cluster jewel.

  • A cheap and powerful anoint you can get on your amulet early on is Testudo.
  • You want to take all 6 Life Masteries to gain 10% more Life. Make sure to avoid having any kind of Life modifier on your body armour to gain an additional 15% increased maximum Life.
  • A massive damage increase for the build is to reach 100% chance to Poison with your Minions, for this reason you need to get multiple Ghastly Eye Jewels with the modifier "Minions have #% Chance to Poison Enemies on hit". Make sure that at least one of them also has Minions taunt on hit and one with Minions Leech #% of damage as Life.

  • When you get The Jinxed Juju, anoint Death Attunement on it and then respec your skill tree into the "Early Death Attunement" tree.

Mid Tree

  • To massively increase your Damage and Minion Survivability you need to respec into the "Mid" skill tree and get two Mid 8 passive large cluster jewels with the notables Feasting Fiends and Renewal. The Renewal is only there to make sure Feasting Fiends is in the correct position. Additionally you want Strength and Minion's have 2% increased attack and cast speed.

Note: The respec assumes you have a Perfect Warlord to provide you with Endurance charges.

  • With the respec you now want to change your anoint into Golem Commander to increase the maximum number of golems you can summon by 1.
  • To increase the damage and the number of Golems you can summon you need to get the following unique jewels:
    1x The Anima Stone
    1x Primordial Might (If this one is very expensive get another Harmony)
    1-3x Primordial Harmony (This reduces the cooldown for your Summon Carrion Golem of Scavenging's skills)
    0-1x Primordial Eminence (This becomes less useful later)

Note: Now when you have increased your Maximum number of summoned golems by 2, you want to change your Summon Carrion Golem of Hordes gem into the more powerful Summon Carrion Golem of Scavenging.

  • You want to use the unique jewel called Watcher's Eye to increase your Physical damage reduction.

Note: You can have other modifiers on it but you want to prioritize the Physical damage reduction modifier when you have Determination.

  • Get 5-6 Tattoo of the Valako Shieldbearer to cap your Attack Block Chance.
  • Use 5-7 Tattoo of the Hinekora Warmonger to boost your Minion's survivability. (These tattoos are also used to manage your Strength vs Intelligence when you have Rational Doctrine)
  • Improve your Ghastly Eye Jewels to the following:
    Mid with taunt
    Option D
    Option E

Note: You don't need an Abyss Jewel with Minion leech now since you have two Feasting Fiends notables on your large clusters.

  • One of the biggest changes in the Mid game setup is to respec your Mistress of Sacrifice ascendancy node into the Lycia Bloodline notable Bitter Heresy. When this is done you need to get a Rational Doctrine jewel and make sure your Strength is higher than your Intelligence.
  • When you have respecced into Bitter Heresy you need to make up for the lost block chance when you used Mistress of Sacrifice together with Bone Offering. Solve this by switching to the "Mid Bitter Heresy" skill tree.

Late Tree

  • Continue scaling the build with these jewels and uniques:
    Amanamu's Gaze to massively increase the Poison damage your Minions deal.
    Late 8 Passive (Minion Damage, Strength and Maximum Life)
    Late 12 passive (Minion Damage, Resistances and Maximum Life)
    Late 4-5 passive (Minion Survivability, Minion Leech and Strength)
    Watcher's Eye (Physical damage reduction and attack block chance)

Note: To be able to fit the new jewels you need to upgrade your Boots and Gloves to have the modifier "Has 1 Abyssal Socket".

  • You need more Tattoos of the Valako Shieldbearer to cap your attack block chance.
  • Replace your Primordial Eminence with one Primordial Harmony. Golem cooldown recovery is more important at this point over golem attack speed.

Additional upgrades can be made in the Late Option 1 & Late Option 2 skill trees. Option 1 has much higher minion survivability than Option 2, but Option 2 increases your own survivability massively by using a Svalinn shield.

  • Use Forbidden Flame and Forbidden Flesh with one of the following modifiers:
    1. Mistress of Sacrifice (Option 1)
    2. Unnatural Strength (Option 1)
    3. Bone Barrier (Option 1)
    4. Liege of the Primordial (Option 2 only, can be very expensive)

Buy the cheapest combination.
If you get a pair for Unnatural Strength or Bone Barrier, then you simply respec the ascendancy into Mistress of Sacrifice.

Note: To fit these two extra jewels you need to upgrade your Late trigger wand into a Late Abyss wand. Remove More Duration Support from your gem setup.

How to Ascend

Learn how to find the Trials of Ascendancy and complete The Lord's Labyrinth to earn your Ascensions with our Labyrinth Guide!

  • Commander of Darkness is an Aura that helps you cap your resistances, and those of your Minions while providing damage. It's a great early game choice, but later you swap it out because you can make up for the damage and resistances with gear.
  • Mindless Aggression is taken since it is on the path toward your next two ascendancy notables.
  • Unnatural Strength converts your Minions Physical damage to Chaos damage. This means all your sources of damage are scaled by the withered on hit effect and Void Manipulation Support.
  • Bone Barrier gives you passive Life recovery as your Minions deal damage. In addition it provides you with Physical damage reduction and gives a significant bump to your Minion's survivability.

Early Game Respec:

In the Early Game when you have more resistances on your items, you respec from Commander of Darkness to Mistress of Sacrifice. Replace Flesh Offering with Bone Offering now.

Mid Game Respec:
In the Mid Game when you have Rational Doctrine, respec from Mistress of Sacrifice to using the Lycia Bloodline Bitter Heresy.

Ascendancy
Pantheon

For Pantheons you take Soul of Gruthkul and Soul of Lunaris. When doing pinnacle bosses that are alone, it is a good idea to swap from Lunaris to Solaris.

Resistances

You can get Resistances wherever you want and they do not have to be on specific items. Resistances are incredibly important though. DO NOT forget about them when choosing which items to craft or buy! Make sure to check your Resistances in your Character Sheet (C, Defense Tab) whenever you switch your gear around. To learn more about defences in Path of Exile, check out our guide here!

Gear in this section is easier to craft if you obtain a Fractured base. This base 'locks' a modifier, so you can use  Essences  and other crafting methods to obtain a good item. You can search for fractured modifiers on trade by inputting "~fractured (name of modifier)" in the modifier search box. If you want to try out how to craft your final items, the Craft of Exile simulator is an amazing tool to plan them without wasting in-game currency.

Item bases

You want to focus on using either Hybrid Armour and Energy Shield or Pure Energy Shield bases.
Armour improves your defensive layer against Physical damage while Energy Shield increases the total damage you can take from all sources except Chaos damage.

  • Upgrade your The Covenant to one with two corrupted implicits. +2 Minion skill levels is mandatory while the second can be one out of a few good options.
