3.27 league start Turbo Leap Slam uber lab farmer

Overview: This build uses toxic rain ballista totems and over scales attack speed to allow for a weapon swap to achieve very fast leap slams. It's entire purpose is to farm Uber lab and leap slam as quickly as possible.

Pros:
-Very fast lab runner
-very cheap to get going on Uber lab farming.
-can do early atlas progression

Cons:
-leap slam is hard to control and can get stuck on walls.
-you have to weapon swap to go really fast
-clunky delayed skill on a totem makes it even more delayed.
-Must be level 70 for build to take off, and level 78 to not have to stance dance for onslaught.
-not good at other non-lab mechanics.

POB: https://maxroll.gg/poe/pob/07jbq0ap

Build Mechanics:

Wilma's requital makes increases to cast speed also apply to attack speed. For some sources this results in double dipping. These sources include
1. Onslaught
2. Haste
3. Frenzy charges
4. Some passives on the tree
5. Possibly adrenaline?

Without a great way to keep frenzy charges up during lab I decided to just not use them.

Wither:
we apply wither with a 5-link totem setup of
Galvanic arrow / ballista totem support / multiple totems support / withering touch / barrage support

This entire 5-link could be replaced by a balance of terror with "inflict withered on hit if you have cast despair in the last 10 seconds", but this is a much cheaper option.


How do I make currency farming Uber Lab?

All you have to do is buy the right gems so that you can profit from the various crafts available. Poe.ninja is a great tool for figuring out which h gems work best for which crafts. These are the crafts in the order I would prioritize them.

1. Exchange a Support Gem for a random Exceptional Gem
2. Transform a non-Transfigured Skill Gem to be a random Transfigured version
3. Sacrifice a Gem to gain #% of the gem's total experience stored as a
Facetor's Lens
4. Add +#% quality to a Gem
5. Transform a Skill Gem to be a random Transfigured Gem of the same colour
6. adding experience to a gem, treasure keys, and currency are not worth it unless you want to level your own gems.

Wich gems to use
1. any support gem, does not matter which kind or its level/quality
2. you want a skill gem that has only 1 transfigured version to guarentee a hit. You also want to have it be level 20 with 20 quality already because those stats stay when you craft it.
3. any level 20 skill or support gem will work, with async trade it's easy to nab these for 1c ea. quality does not matter, but buying 0 quality makes it cost less gold.
4. You want a gem that adding quality to adds a lot of value. buy a level 20 version and then use this to add quality. the values are 8/10/15/20 so make sure you combine them in a way that doesn't waste crafts. 3 8% quality, 2 10% quality, 8+15% quality, or 1 20% quality.
5. Figure out which transfigured gem color has the most hits and keep around level 20 with 20 quality gems of the same color to random roll with. If you don't like the outcomes you can cancel and spend the craft, or you can keep the one you pick to use later on the same craft.

Guestimated values:
1. Rare craft outlier, not really worth considering but a nice bonus when you hit enlighten
2. You will probably hit this a couple of times during an hour the value varies greatly depending on which gems are popular that league, but they generally sell for around 100c to a div and the 20/20 gems cost somewhere around 30-40c
3. the smaller one (30%) is worth about 15-20 chaos and the larger(60%) is about 30-40 chaos
4. Quality is super common and one of the best money makers in lab, a full 20 quality nets you about 40-45 chaos so the 8% is 13 chaos, 10% is 20 chaos and 20% is 40 chaos
5. Totally random, sometiems it's junk 20-30c alleged value gems that never sell and sometimes you hit the meta transfigured gem that has multiple targets that sell for 200c.
Most uber lab runs take 4-5 minutes to complete meaning you will easily be able to run 10 labs per hour which is 20 uses of the divine font for gem crafting.
