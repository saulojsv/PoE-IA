About the Build

Absolution been a very popular as a leveling skill before and after a big set of buffs in 3.28 it finally makes a proper comeback.

Now Absolution Guardian Build is pretty easy to play and setup and it works very good while leveling. Build can also scale to endgame very good since Doryani's Prototype provides A LOT of DPS.

As defenses, you will have a endurance Charges, High block and 90% Resistances in the Doryanis Version.

We can also use Corruption Embrace chaos ascendency to have almost 100% uptime of additional Vaal Absolution minion to improve the DPS even further.

Eldritch Battery will fix all mana issues too and will allow us worry free casts.

It’s a chill League starter build that’s easy to play and not very expensive to setup.

▸Important Item Stats

The stat that will give you the most damage is + Level to minion skills. Make sure to have it on Wand, shield and Helmet.

Amulet

Early on you can just use a Rare Amulet with +1 To Lightning Skill Gems or Physical Skill Gems.

Some Life also will be a bonus. Later on Replica Dragon Flight Amulet with +3 levels for absolution will be a great choice if you have the budget.

Wand

  • Aim to get the +1to all spell or Lightning or Physical skills at your wand.
  • The rest of the bonuses should be for minion damage. Crit and Speed is higher priority.
  • ❗Make sure that you have an open Suffix to craft Trigger a Socketed Spell when you Use a Skill at the bench.

Armour Items

  • Focus on getting high Armour on your armour items, at least a few of those also should have hybrid ES so you can use Eldritch Battery to cast your Absolution. So hybrid Armour / ES is perfect.
  • Get life where you can.
  • Doryani's Prototype will be great later on, look for more info about when to swap to it down below.

Rings

  • Before Doryani's Prototype swap you will need minion rings with some resistances and life.
  • After the swap, aim for the rings with - Lightning resistance to rech -200% Lightning
  • Rest of the stats should be Life, resistances or Minion damage if your budget is high.

▸Bandits

  • Get rid of all or Help Alira early on. Will need to swap it later in that case.

▸Defences

Block

A key defensive layer for Absolution Guardian build.

Invest into Sanctuary and Safeguard when you can.

Endurance charges

To improve survivability it is very important to add Small Cluster Jewel with Enduring Composure ASAP. It will provide Endurance charges in maps. try to get it between lvl 70 and 90 if you can.

Later on, when you will have higher Budget - it is possible to sue Impossible Escape jewel to Blood Magic and allocate Inexorable instead. Such approach can be expensive but it can also boost block since you can get As The Mountain as well.

Flesh and Stone

Flesh and Stone is a great defensive aura for Absolution Build - so always keep it in SAND Stance.

▸ How to cap Chaos?

Since we are using Divine Flesh after the Doryanis swap, it will be pretty important to cap chaos resistance.

Roll chaos res on most of the items and use The Light of Meaning (Chaos Resistance) if you need it as well.

▸ Anoints

Use Whispers of Doom to curse enemies with Elemental Weakness + Conductivity BEFORE Doryani's Prototype swap.

AFTER the swap, use Assassin's Mark + Punishment instead.

❗When to swap to Doryani's Prototype and HOW to do that:

So when you will swap to Doryani's Prototype your lightning resistances will be negative. To make sure that Lightning damage is not killing you, you will need to have the following:

1. Glorious Vanity (Xibaqua Divine Flesh) that provides Divine Flesh keystone passive. You will place it in the top socket so it will replace Hex Master.

If it will have passive for Chaos Resistance / Max Chaos resistance it will be even better.

❗Watch this video with timestamp to see how to search for the jewel - https://youtu.be/Z4tVWAIqOkE?si=OnR5F1vfp5ijMzZR&t=870

2. Dawnbreaker shield with at least 18-20% of Lightning as Fire and 18-20% of Cold as Fire.

If you can afford it try to get all Dawnbreaker rolls as close to 20% as possible.

3. Watcher's Eye Jewel with - Cold and Lightning Damage taken as Fire while affected by Purity of Fire.

You will also need to use Purity of Fire itself.

If you have Glorious Vanity + Shield but don't have Watcher's you can still swap but try to get Watcher's ASAP. You will also need to get rid of Lighting resistance from all items and get Rings with -Lightning resistance from trade.

Look into Ring Section above for more recommendations.

What Spectres AND AG to use:

❗Watch this video for Types / Items - https://youtu.be/Peqi0MCjGlo

Always buy Tiger LAST because it is expensive.

In the gloves we have a supportive setup with additional minions.

It can be Spectres, Golem or Animated Guardian if you have a budget for it.

Spectres can provide strong buffs so it is recommended to use those.

❗If you are not familiar with Spectres, unsure what spectres to use or just a beginner, just use the golem instead.


What if you don't have enough Mana:

Since we are using energy shield to cast with Eldritch Battery, you will need only 1 mana to play.

However, if for any reason you don't have enough - dropping Tempest Shield or Flesh and Stone is a valid option, so feel free to do that.

What Curse to use:

Before Doryani's Prototype you will need to use Elemental Weakness + Conductivity to improve the damage of your Absolution minions and your Guardian of Radiance.

After you will acquire Doryani's Prototype and your Lightning res will be negative, Assassin's Mark and Punishment will work much better instead.
