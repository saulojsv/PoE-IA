[3.25] Hollow Palm Cyclone Build | Slayer | Settlers of Kalguur | Path of Exile 3.25

Hey guys, hollow palm cyclone slayer build is here. Using The Dancing Dervish to get rampage and onslaught all the time.


Build Cost
- Medium Budget


What is Hollow Palm Technique?
Hollow Palm Technique is a keystone passive skill which makes the player character count as Dual Wielding while Unencumbered. Basically we can't have any weapon, shield or gloves but we gain these stats:
- 40% more Attack Speed while you are Unencumbered
- 14 to 20 Added Attack Physical Damage per 10 Dexterity while you are Unencumbered

Brutal Restraint: You can see the mods you get from timeless jewels on path of building now. Try to get one with at least two "5% increased Dexterity" mod.

Precise Tecnique: 40% more Attack Damage if Accuracy Rating is higher than Maximum Life
- Make sure your accuracy rating is higher than your life.

Onslaught: While The Dancing Dervishes are manifested, the player character is considered unarmed and is affected by Onslaught.

Leveling: You can start using hollow palm tecnique at level 12. Start with ground slam and keep playing until like +60 level. Cyclone is not really good for leveling. Leveling gems and trees are in the video.

Leveling Uniques:
- Tabula Rasa
- Le Heup of All
- Karui Ward, Astramentis
- Seven-League Step
- Goldrim
- Belt of the Deceiver

Build Video: https://youtu.be/a0OJu4KJHWs

Bandits: Kill All

Gem Setup:

Blood and Sand / Flesh and Stone / Pride / Determination

The Dancing Dervish:
Maim / Blind / Increased Area of Effect / Faster Attacks / Minion Speed / Item Rarity
