[3.25] Splitting Steel Gladiator - All Uniques, Cheap League Start, Explody Mapping

"
dreais wrote:

Playing attack, and especially melee, is different compared to spells. You mostly played spell based chars that got to endgame or followed builds for attack ones. But you did make mistakes on the splitting steel

1. you have 2 banners, only one can be used at a time
2. No totem. That's a huge dps loss
3. No herald of purity. Big dps loss
4. GMP. The overlap is minimal at the early stages, you should go for Vicious Proj first. You shouldn't mind the extra proj for now.
5. Blood rage. If you have enough life sustain, good dps boost + frenzy during clearing
Also early, you need to craft with vendor recipe the highest dps base that you can find, be it 2h axe or 2h sword. Yours is good enough but you could go with a vendor recipe + AS craft


TL;DR, the build is functional, it just might not be for you at the moment

EDIT: i forgot one main point: the gem hasn't been buffed yet.

Thanks for the comments.

1. I tend to have 'spare' gems in slots to level them up just in case I find a use for them later, or want to switch or whatever. Same deal with determination here, cause I don't have the mana to run that + grace. Probably a few others too. Or in weapon swap.

2. True, I don't have totems, and it's a big dps loss I suppose. But I just don't like the totem / ballista playstyle. If it was a minion that followed me around, it would be different, but needing to place these support wheels every few seconds is frustrating. Fine in boss fights cause you're in an arena, but otherwise it's a pain. Wish GGG changed that and baked damage into gems, but oh well... maybe next league, huh?

3. Fair point, maybe I should have used that instead. Herald of Ash was nice at times, but due to the pitiful damage it only really procced on white mobs, where it wasn't all that needed anyway.

4. GMP. I play on PS5, so can't easily check dps in PoB or whatever, and picked this up cause it looked like a good fit. Sounds like it wasn't.

5. Sure, blood rage would be nice, but I'm always a bit concerned by the degen from it. Good for juggs and such, but yeah. Didn't think about blood rage here, but perhaps it would have been a good option.

6. I actually did craft with that recipe. Not on the current weapon I think, cause it was better than the one I crafted later, so didn't swap to it. But I had made a rule in my filter (filterblade.xyz) to look for 2-handed swords, so eventually would have upgraded with something better.

Just wanted to comment back to try to explain things a little bit. I usually have a bit of a mess of gems and sockets while levelling, cause it's hard to get what you ideally want. Played in SSF here to try to mimic a league start scenario, so that's why I couldn't spam fuses or whatever to get what I would have wanted. From my practice runs lately, just getting a suitable 4-link can be real rough. RNG is often a mean mistress!

Anyway, best of luck with this build. I'll probably have it in the back of my mind, maybe try it at some point, but don't think I dare to league start with it given this experience during the first half of the campaign. Killing stuff, especially bosses, took way too long. Sure, I could have made better choices, but it wouldn't have made a seismic difference I think. Have seen quite a few people say that levelling with splitting steel is kinda torturous. Hopefully less so this league with the changes, but that remains to be seen I suppose. People say it scales really bad with gem levels, but at high levels it scales really well. Makes for a rough campaign.
