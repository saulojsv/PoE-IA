Smite Champion(doryani armor stack)



so i used the character for sanctum, hence the double replicas.



there isnt mana siphoner at sanctum so we can lvl100 and the chest itself coupled wtih reflected jewelery triples our dps.



alphas howl with mana corrupt is really hard to beat. you can get some life + more dmg from crafting a helmet, but less mana reserve and it will be costly.



celestial brace doesnt need to be perfect and you can use a rare initially if youre short on money or resists. its a very nice dps boost once you can equip it tho.



reflected jewelery. max lightning res is capped at 200. try to get some other good stats - life, other resist, intelligence.



you can use perseverance belt with balbala timeless jewel for flask generation - you wont need onslaught flask so youll get charge generation for 2 empty flask slots.



rest of the clusters and passive tree jewels.



relics. at some point i go for +4,5 double merchant, but any combination can work especially early. overall after the latest nerfs, sanctum drops pretty hard in terms of currency generation after the first week or two.
