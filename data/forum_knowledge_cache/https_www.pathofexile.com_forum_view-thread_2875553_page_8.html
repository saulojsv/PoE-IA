Razunter wrote:
I don't get why did you remove the warcry, you can just put it on the left click when it's instant and forget about it.

I removed it because from the feedbacks I got here and in game, I learned out that some players just can't play with it or use it in a wrong way which slows down clear speed. There I decided to look for other ways to play with it for simplicity and easiness, because this is one of the things this build promises. So it had to become easier. There I came up with numpad trick and Blood Magic idea and that ended up with fail, because GGG's support refused to share info about numpad trick's availability in this game(here). I also thought about putting it on left click, but we move with Leap like %95 of the time so it'd mostly be active on bosses that way only when we walk while doing them, again wasn't a good solution. So I decided to sacrifice DPS for easiness and simplicity and changed it back to Increased Duration. I'm thinking to add Berserk there for both offensive and defensive improvement on bosses, but first I want to see the availability of cluster jewels sometime after the patch, because it's tied to Bloodscent there. That's where I will decide what to do with cluster jewel on Required Gear as well. Meta Gear's cluster will stay the same.

Hi. I wrote down steps of crafting the axe on Meta Gear before in this thread as a reply to Arukabe, I'm copy pasting it. "Axe on meta gear is best crafted on a Siege Axe because of the base attack speed it has. Your base has to be Elder influenced for the Culling Strike mod. Do alt/aug until you get one of the prefixes and the Culling Strike suffix then imprint beast(Craicic Chimeral) it and regal it. If you get a suffix you need to do bestiary's "Add a Prefix, Remove a Random Suffix". If it fails, use the imprint on the weapon to restore it back to blue, and repeat the process. If it fails again, buy another Craicic Chimeral and start from the beginning. Good luck!"

Though I have to say I haven't delved into harvest mechanics a lot(Spent most of my free time testing and improving the build on standard), so hopefully somebody here can help with that and make this crafting process easier with harvest mechanics.
