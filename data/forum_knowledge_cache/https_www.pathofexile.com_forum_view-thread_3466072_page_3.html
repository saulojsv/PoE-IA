JURITO1000 wrote:
heya,what charms to use?

First of all, you CAN actually play this build with Maji - 25% inc. life from not using gloves combined with a +2 tincture is def. a very potent option that is easier to optimize than primalist.

With that out of the way, modifiers u want to prioritize:

- 0.X critial strike chance while on max Power charges
- no extra dmg from critial strikes while elusive
- chance to gain power charge on crit strike

With those covered, there is a huge amount of benefitial mods, including crit chance/multi against burning/shocked enemies, chance to gain phasing on kill, endu charges when hit, cannot be stunned while leeching, shock from hits always increase dmg taken by at least X, chance to create conq ground, conq. Ground increases dmg taken by at least X..

So you basically want to cover the mandatory modifiers and then fill up based on your preferences and target farm. Bossing means endu charge on hit and stun immunity only are "okayish", conq. Ground dmg increase is rather wasted for mapping,etc.

Ah yes, and then there is obviously the +1 proj charm if you so desire to put like 50div into a single charm mod (spoiler: Unless extra minmaxing I do not consider this worth it)
