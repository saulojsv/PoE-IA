Basically Accuracy affects flat chance to hit and also your crit chance. It has double influence so it's good to have it capped.
Helmet might be the cheapest slot to have accuracy.
You can also skip accuracy on gear by using enlighten support lvl 4 gem and raising lvl of Precision, but it's quite expensive. Not really worth.

Fan of Blades cluster is hard to make so price is justified.

Take your time. Build has enough power to carry you along the way.
