[3.25 SSC] 36 charges Penance Brand Slayer VS EndGame - VIDEO UP

Hello guys, here a quick showcase of a charge stacker slayer penance brand.

The build is really solid thanks to change to endurance charges and lucky block; and as you can see the dps is not a problem at all.
This build can be also upgraded in defence, changing some setups and using triple elemental flask.
About DPS is hard to get more, also a mirror wand has low chance to beat a void battery with 10% ele pen corruption. (I also miss a mirror ring with spell per power and more multi but I didn't want to invest so much in this build).
In opinion, after seeing inquisitor and trickster version, slayer remain the best choice for it.

This build is much better in single target than clear speed, i help the clear with herald of ash + sirus's flask.

Anyway nothing to say anymore i think.
If you have any tips or question feel free to write here in thread.

Enjoy ^_^
