3.21 Gear update, Video update & Reaction to Palsteron's latest video

Hi Guys,

So, I just watched Palsteron's latest video and wanted to take a moment to talk about it, because I think what he said captures some of my feelings and some of the philosophy that goes into my builds.

If you haven't watched it, here's the video:

YouTube: 3.21 Palsteron - (The ACTUAL Reason You Make No Currency in Path of Exile)

A few examples of things he mentions that I feel apply to this build:

* Haste instead of Determination
* High Attack Speed/Mobility
* Just enough defensive layers to make farming viable (the best defense is a good offense)
* 20% Deli farming, not 100%
* Couldn't give less of a shit about Uber Ubers viability, if ur good the build can certainly do them
* Less focus on PoB numbers and more on how the build feels in-game
* If you don't know what you're doing, always follow a guide

Now, with that little diatribe out of the way, I want to show the latest changes I've made, and then a showcase video of what I usually do, which is 20% deli Crimson Temple farming.

Here's the latest gear:



PoB Export: https://pobb.in/lgJjWHrTcKvO

Changes:

Biggest one is Ashes, which is a massive DPS boost, once you have all the alternate gems that can take advantage of the +Q bonus, and of course the Mana Reservation is nice, but not strictly required as we have a more than adequate Mana pool without it.

Breathstealer Gauntlets take the place of Tanu Ahi, so that we can anoint them with "Vengeant Cascade" and Ashes with "Whispers of Doom", as getting a +1 Curse Ashes is pretty much impossible/insanely expensive. They are also amazing for mapping with good density, as the Spores they create can give you random Auras, including those from Monster Mods.

Crucible tree Replica Wings which gives us a nice DPS boost. Bought for 5div.

Death Rush takes the place of Tanu Ahi to still allow us to gain Adrenaline while mapping. For bossing I just keep it on, DPS is more than enough already to melt pretty much everything, but if you really really want to melt shit, you can put on two Circle's of Guilt.

Here's the video:

YouTube: 3.21 Crimson Temple - (20% Deli Mapping Showcase, +50% Monster Life)

As you can see, you make plenty of loot. Just run a single cheap deli orb, some scarabs of your choice, some sextants, doesn't matter much...

One thing I've forgotten to mention, especially for the newer players, is the use of Sinvicta's as a secondary weapon. You just weapon swap, kill a mob to start Rampage and then switch back to Replica Wings and keep killing to sustain it. As you can see, sustaining 1000 stacks is not hard, and regaining Rampage, should you lose it, is just a weapon swap/1-kill to restart.

One last tip; if you're low on Sextants, give Delve Depth 145+ a shot:
