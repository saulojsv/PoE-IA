Viper strike of Mamba Pathfinder League Starter for Path of Exile Mirage 3.28

by Crouching_Tuna
Mirage 3.28Last Updated: March 25, 2026

Advertisement

The Viper Strike of the Mamba Pathfinder offers a powerful and adaptable League Start Poison build, characterized by strong clear speed and robust defenses. The Viper Strike of the Mamba skill sums up the main-hand and off-hand damage of equipped weapons, maximizing potential strike damage. This benefit is counterbalanced by a limitation to a single poison application per enemy.

Unlike other poison skills that heavily incentivize maximizing uptime, duration, and attack speed for a gradual damage ramp, Viper Strike of the Mamba front-loads its damage resulting in a significantly snappier feel.

The Pathfinder Ascendancy notable, Master Toxicist, bypasses the single poison application limitation of Viper Strike of the Mamba. As you kill weaker enemies within the pack poisons proliferate through the group stacking up on stronger foes like Rares and Unique Bosses. This allows the build to perform in mechanics such as Breach, Expedition, Ritual, Essence, Blight, and Harvest.

Early itemization prioritizes hybrid scaling of Life, Energy Shield, and Evasion to enhance survivability. Master Surgeon provides continuous life recovery, which mitigates the downside of Petrified Blood, allowing you to recover life beyond 50%, granting 22% damage reduction. While this build operates without mandatory unique items, The Sorrow of the Divine allows life flask effects to extend to Energy Shield, doubling your recovery. This in combination with Petrified Blood, and Fortification granted by Fortify Support results in a build that is deceptively tanky.

Resistances

You do not need specific resistances on any item, just get the required total resistances amongst all gear combined. You need to cap your Resistances at 75% unless otherwise specified in the guide!

From levels 12 to 33, Poisonous Concoction is used to apply multiple poison stacks to enemies via projectile overlap, dealing Chaos Damage over time. In place of using, or upgrading your weapon you instead gain additional chaos damage by converting the life recovery of Divine Life Flask, resulting in significant damage output with minimal cost.

The most efficient leveling strategy involves continuing with Poisonous Concoction until you obtain enough Chaos Orbs to trade for the Viper Strike of the Mamba gem from another player, then performing the swap. If you wish to follow a step-by-step campaign progression guide for Poisonous concoction check our FAQ. Alternatively, players who prefer to transition to their endgame skill immediately can farm the Normal Labyrinth to acquire the Viper Strike of the Mamba gem by socketing a green gem into the font.

You can swap to Viper Strike of the Mamba immediately upon completing the first Labyrinth and allocating Master Toxicist. Until you obtain Nature's Adrenaline, alternate between flasks when clearing to maximise the poison spread provided by Master Toxicist. Make sure to do 'A Fixture of Fate' in Act 3, to get Added Chaos Damage Support, Battlemage's Cry, Autoexertion, Ancestral Cry and Fortify Support.

Use Battlemage's Cry linked to Autoexertion to trigger Alchemist's Mark. This is required to transition to Viper Strike of the Mamba.

Ancestral Cry can be socketed with your existing Autoexertion setup or in a separate item with another Autoexertion gem.

After allocating Whispers of Doom on the passive skill tree, link Despair can be linked to your Battlemage's Cry setup to automate your second curse too.

Withering Step applies the Withered debuff to enemies, increasing chaos damage taken, use this against Rares and Bosses.

Reserve Herald of Agony for early damage. Transition to Flesh and Stone when you swap to Viper Strike of the Mamba as you should be in melee from now on.

Purity of Elements grants a significant increase in elemental resistances and immunity to all elemental ailments.

This Viper Strike of the Mamba stage uses Pneumatic Daggers, gaining the ability to poison with elemental damage. You use Awakened Added Cold Damage Support or other flat elemental damage gems for additional DPS.

Pneumatic Daggers simplify mid-tier weapon crafting, as they permit the use of Deafening Essence of Hatred or Deafening Essence of Rage to more readily acquire a high-damage weapon compared to physical or hybrid physical/chaos swords. Additionally, during weapon crafting, an open prefix modifier can be utilized to craft 'Hits Can't be Evaded,' eliminating the need to itemize or allocate passive skill points for accuracy.

Pneumatic Daggers simplify mid-tier weapon crafting, spam Deafening Essence of Hatred or Deafening Essence of Rage to more easily acquire a high-damage weapon compared to physical or hybrid physical/chaos swords. Craft ‘Hits Can’t be Evaded’ to eliminate the need for accuracy on gear or passives.

Before swapping Purity of Elements for Malevolence, you need boots with 100% chance to Avoid being Shocked, and the 100% chance to Avoid being Chilled craft. In addition, upgrade the Brine King Pantheon with soul of Captain Tanner Lightfoot. This results in immunity to Shock, Chill, and Freeze, but you remain vulnerable to Scorch, Brittle, and Sap.

Skill gem changes are minimal compared to previous versions, notably the inability to scale from elemental damage, unlike the Pneumatic Daggers variant, forcing you to drop Awakened Added Cold Damage Support.

The mana variant of Viper Strike of the Mamba utilizes the Whispers of Infinity amulet to scale added flat chaos damage through Mana stacking. The Whispers of Infinity amulet also converts mana costs into Energy Shield costs, eliminating the need for mana cost reduction and mana leech. Both Mana and Pneumatic Daggers variants offer comparable damage at a mid-tier budget. However, the mana variant has superior scaling at higher investments due to its capacity to gain damage from Mana prefix modifiers on items and accessible passive skills.

Double check all of the skill gems, gear, and passive tree variants for each progression step to make sure you have enough mana for all your reservations.

Leveling Trees

  • Use Poisonous Concoction tree variant until you obtain Viper Strike of the Mamba. Then, transition to the Mamba Early variant. For a step-by-step campaign progression guide dedicated to Poisonous Concoction, refer to our FAQ.
  • If needed, head to Faustus to respec for a gold cost. This is very cost efficient during the campaign.
    • Note: Once you allocate Whispers of Doom, ensure Despair is included in your Autoexertion, Battlemage's Cry gem link setup.
    • Charisma enables the reservation of your Autoexertion setups, Purity of Elements, and Petrified Blood. Allocating Leader of the Pack allows for the inclusion of Flesh and Stone at higher level.
    • Look for dexterity, or dexterity intelligence bases to activate the Evasion Mastery that provides "15% chance to suppress spell damage if equipped body armour, helmet, gloves and boots all have evasion rating".

Mamba Mid & Endgame Trees

You begin using these variants once you begin using Low Tolerance. These clusters should be an early priority, as they each provide substantial damage relative to other equipped gear, excluding weapons. Flesh and Stone can be removed in this variant until you obtain your Reservation Efficiency cluster. If you have any spare passive points, you can socket and spec into an Enduring Composure cluster.

  • Only apply the Runegraft of Treachery onto the Evasion and Energy Shield Mastery once you have swapped Purity of Elements for Malevolence, otherwise, you struggle to maintain your fortify. This allows you to drop the Reservation Efficiency Cluster.
  • The Glorious Vanity must specify 'sacrificed in the name of Doryani.' The numerical value is inconsequential unless min-maxing, in which case refer to our timeless jewel calculator guide. Position this jewel near the Elemental Equilibrium keystone and allocate it to gain 15% of Maximum Life as Extra Maximum Energy Shield.
  • The Light of Meaning is a powerful, jewel that you socket into the Ranger jewel socket. It provides a large amount of Chaos Resistance, reducing gear pressure required to cap.

  • Sufficient +#% to Fire, Cold, Lightning and Chaos Resistance to cap your Resistances.

  • % Chance to Suppress Spell Damage
  • Appropriately rolled Flasks
  • Flask Charges Gained
  • Evasion Rating.

Resistances

You can get Resistances wherever you want and they do not have to be on specific items. Resistances are incredibly important though. DO NOT forget about them when choosing which items to craft or buy! Make sure to check your Resistances in your Character Sheet (C, Defense Tab) whenever you switch your gear around. To learn more about defences in Path of Exile, check out our guide here!

Gear in this section is easier to craft if you obtain a Fractured base. This base 'locks' a modifier, so you can use  Essences  and other crafting methods to obtain a good item. You can search for fractured modifiers on trade by inputting "~fractured (name of modifier)" in the modifier search box. If you want to try out how to craft your final items, the Craft of Exile simulator is an amazing tool to plan them without wasting in-game currency.

Weapons

When leveling with Viper Strike of the Mamba Your Weapons are your most important upgrade! Always keep your Weapons up to date within ~7 levels of yourself. Make sure your Weapon either has +% Physical Damage or +# to Physical Damage. Vendoring a weapon (any rarity) with a Magic or Rare Rustic Sash (Rare is better) and a Blacksmith's Whetstone returns the same base type weapon with #% Increased Physical Damage on it. It's recommended you do this as soon as you get your first and keep upgrading using this recipe throughout the acts.

Jewels

Given that the Viper Strike of the Mamba skill gem is limited to a maximum of one poison per target, the Low Tolerance cluster jewel notable provides significant value. Prioritizing these clusters early is a good way to gain a huge DPS increase. A second notable is not required for these clusters to be effective. They can also be temporarily socketed into Large Cluster Jewels until the specific jewels detailed in this guide can be crafted or purchased.

Item Bases

Focus on itemizing with Hybrid Evasion and Energy Shield bases. Evasion provides attack damage avoidance, enabling consistent recovery between hits. Energy Shield increases your health pool against damage from all sources except Chaos damage.

The following are the optimal bases, though they possess high level requirements. Equip the highest level base available and upgrade frequently during progression to bolster your defences:

  • Necrotic Armour
  • Torturer's Mask
  • Phantom Boots
  • Phantom Mitts

Flasks

Pathfinder is an Ascendancy that maximizes flask utility by providing flask charge generation, ensuring consistent uptime. This enables builds that are reliant on their flasks for offense and defense. Master Surgeon allows life flask effects to persist beyond full life. A high recovery flask such as Saturated Divine Life Flask of Assuaging delivers a constant flow of life recovery. The Sorrow of the Divine extends life flask recovery to your Energy Shield, doubling the total recovery.

Taste of Hate converts a significant portion of incoming Fire and Lightning damage taken to Cold and provides maximum Cold Resistance. This lets you fit a Experimenter's Quicksilver Flask of the Cheetah and a Perpetual Silver Flask of the Owl for increased speed. For a more defensive flask configuration, these can be replaced with a Alchemist's Ruby Flask of the Owl, an Alchemist's Sapphire Flask of the Impala, and a Topaz Flask.

During the campaign, try to get Life and Resistances on every non-weapon item. But make sure to prioritize gem links on items as well.

Equip a Sapphire Ring for Merveil.

Vendoring an Iron Ring with a red, green or blue gem gives you a normal rarity Ruby Ring, Topaz Ring or Sapphire Ring respectively.

Use an Amber Amulet to ensure you have enough Strength to equip gems and items.

You can use the crafting bench to craft any extra Resistances for 1 Orb of Transmutation, this works on normal rarity items as well.

If you need extra Strength or Dexterity, you can use the crafting bench for 1 Orb of Augmentation to add what you need.

Equip Two-Stone Rings after level 20, and get Fire, Cold, and Lightning Resistances as close to 75+ as possible.

Prepare a pair of Early Swords with as much maximum base physical damage for your current level to facilitate a smooth transition to Viper Strike of the Mamba.

Prioritize getting a 6 link body armour with as much Evasion and Energy Shield as possible early on Early 6l, it is important to not have any Life modifiers on your body armour because of the Life Mastery "15% increased maximum Life if there are no Life modifiers on equipped body armour".

Correct gem links are crucial, as your Autoexertion, Battlemage's Cry, and Alchemist's Mark setup doubles your damage. Upon allocating Whispers of Doom, ensure Despair is included in this socket group.

At item level 83, one-handed swords gain access to the 'Adds # to # Chaos Damage' modifier. Use of the Harvest bench to guarantee this modifier when reforging for chaos modifiers on an ilevel 83 Legion Sword Base. This process costs 100 Vivid Crystallised Lifeforce per attempt but can yield a strong starter weapon with high combined chaos and physical damage.

Crafting a Low Tolerance cluster is your first priority when transitioning to the midgame. This notable rolls on Chaos Damage over Time Medium clusters of a minimum item level of 68. You can expect to roll the notable with 100Orbs of Alteration and Orbs of Augmentation. Each such cluster increases your damage by 20%, and their compounding effect contributes significantly to overall damage. These should be socketed in a Large Cluster Jewel. The secondary modifiers on these clusters are less relevant than Low Tolerance itself, begin using them as soon as you obtain this notable!

A Small Cluster Jewel with the Mortifying Aspect notable enables the removal of Leader of the Pack while still fitting all auras.

For all other rare jewel sockets, prioritize fitting Rare Jewels that address any missing resistances or attributes. These jewels offer both damage increases while solving problems with your current gear.

Before swapping Purity of Elements for Malevolence, you need boots with 100% chance to Avoid being Shocked, and the 100% chance to Avoid being Chilled craft. In addition, upgrade the Brine King Pantheon with soul of Captain Tanner Lightfoot. This results in immunity to Shock, Chill, and Freeze, but you remain vulnerable to Scorch, Brittle, and Sap.

Your Gloves, Boots and Body Armour can all have 2 Eldritch Implicits each. These Implicits can be added by applying Searing Exarch and Eater of Worlds currency.

Eldritch Currency can be found from Altar Rewards in Maps after choosing either the Searing Exarch or Eater of Worlds Beacon on the Map Device. They have tiers, for example, the lowest tier currencies are: Lesser Eldritch Ichor and Lesser Eldritch Ember. If you don't like an outcome, just roll over it with another Eldritch Currency. Don't go overboard on rerolling, you have to redo the Implicits later anyways.

Pneumatic Daggers enable elemental damage scaling for increased poison damage, making it easy to craft powerful weapons with Hatred and Wrath Essences at a relatively low cost. Two high-tier Elemental Damage rolls combined with the 'Hits Can't be Evaded' craft provide a significant damage increase at this stage and should be prioritized.

An Amulet anointed with Sione's Ambition grants a substantial increase in damage and the range of ancestral strikes.

Only apply the Runegraft of Treachery onto the Evasion and Energy Shield Mastery once you have swapped Purity of Elements for Malevolence, or you will struggle to maintain your fortify. This allows you to drop the Reservation Efficiency Cluster for a Small Cluster Jewel with Enduring Composure.

When you can afford it, consider buying a Stormshroud unique jewel. This item provides total ailment immunity, if you have 100% chance to avoid shocks. Deafening Essence of Torment boots, utilizing an Exceptional Eldritch implicit, fulfills this requirement. Note that you can keep an Abyss Jewel socketed, and the chance to avoid being chilled boot craft Pantheon combo from previous stages if you are not yet able to afford it.

The The Tides of Time Unique provides a substantial increase to life recovery from flasks, increased flask effect, and additional flask charges. Prioritize a high roll on the "Utility Flask Gain # Charges" modifier.

Voices jewels are highly impactful for this build, letting you socket additional Low Tolerance clusters. Their utility is contingent on having five or less passive skills that grant nothing.

The Light of Meaning is a powerful, jewel that can be socketed in the Ranger jewel socket. It provides a large amount of Chaos Resistance, reducing gear pressure required to cap.

The Ashes of the Stars unique provides additional quality to Viper Strike of the Mamba, granting 60% more damage with poisons from a 30% quality of All Skill Gems roll. Anoint Ashes of the Stars with Sione's Ambition for a significant increase in damage per second and area clear potential.

The mana variant of Viper Strike of the Mamba utilizes the Whispers of Infinity amulet to scale added flat chaos damage through Mana stacking. The Whispers of Infinity amulet also converts mana costs into Energy Shield costs, eliminating the need for mana cost reduction and mana leech.

Both Mana and Pneumatic Daggers variants offer comparable damage at a mid-tier budget. However, the mana variant has superior scaling at higher investments due to your ability to gain damage from Mana prefix modifiers on items and accessible passive skills.

To increase the chaos damage granted by the Whispers of Infinity, craft Mana Prefixes on any gear piece, such as Gloves. Rings are another great source of Mana.

A Shavronne's Revelation in the right ring slot provides a big increase to Maximum Mana, Intelligence, Energy Shield and damage. Use Fertile Catalyst to further augment the mana modifier by 20%.

Low Tolerance and Eldritch Inspiration jewels contribute significantly to damage output. Crafting this Cluster takes on average 4,000 Orbs of Alteration; Buying one via trade is recommended. If you can't afford it, Prioritize Low Tolerance.

To address mana reservation issues, craft two Small Cluster Jewels. These items can also supply a ton of Energy Shield and attributes.

    • Complete the quests Victario's Secrets, The Ribbon Spool and The Gemling Queen. Choose the rare Lapis Amulet as a reward.
    • Ensure that you complete the Trial of Ascendancy from The Catacombs at some point during this cycle.
    Step 3: Level 26
    • Complete the quest Piety's Pets.
      • Ignore the quest Sever the Right Hand. We do not need to kill Gravicius for this build.
    • Aim for at LEAST level 27 before killing Piety.
      • Farm in The Docks if you are behind in XP. You can reset a zone by holding down Ctrl and left clicking on either a zone entrance or Waypoint.
    Step 4: Level 27
    • While in the Imperial Gardens, go to the Library and complete the side quest A Fixture of Fate.
      • This quest gives access to all gems in the game level 31 and below.
      • Talk to Siosa and purchase Shield Charge and Unbound Ailments Support
        • Shield Charge is now your primary movement skill, link it to Faster Attacks Support.
        • Unbound Ailments Support will replace Onslaught Support, as at this point you should have the passive point Graceful Assault.
    Step 5: Level 27
    • Try to get your resists close to 75% Fire, Cold, and Lightning.
    • The best craft you can make is with the Crafting Bench in your hideout. Use the 16-20% of a single resist craft, which only costs an Orb of Transmutation.
    • Talk to Hargan for a Passive Skillpoint (from the quest Victario's Secrets). Then talk to him again and shop for a four-link by pasting in the regex -[rgb]-.-|g-g-g|nne
    • Talk to Grigor to get a Passive Skillpoint.
    • Complete the Trial of Ascendancy in The Imperial Gardens.
    • While fighting Dominus, avoid getting slammed when he says "The Touch of God!". Kill Dominus and move on to Act 4!
    Step 1: Level 28
    • Complete the quest Breaking the Seal.
    • Farm Dried Lake until at least level 30.
    • Act 4 is when the game strains your resistances. Playing with below 75% elemental resists is extremely dangerous. Start to over-cap resists in this Act, as we are aiming for at least 105% by the time we kill Act 5 Kitava.
    • Keep identifying rare items and keep chugging along with high Life and resists gear.

    • Complete the quest An Indomitable Spirit
    • Reach the Waypoint in the Crystal Veins and complete the normal Labryinth.
    Important

    • Ascend as a Pathfinder, and choose Nature's Reprisal.
      • Nature's Reprisal gives Wither on hit and effect, which synergizes perfectly with Poisonous Concoction.
    Step 3: Level 32
    • Do not progress past Crystal Veins without being at least level 32, farm Dried Lake if you need to catch up.
    • Complete the quest The Eternal Nightmare by killing Daresso and Kaom.
    Step 4: Level 34
    • Kill Piety, then complete the quest Corpus Malachus.
    • Talk to Piety again, go kill Malachai, and move on to Act five!
    Step 1: Level 35
    • Get at least 75% resists in Fire, Cold, and Lightning - but aim for 105%, as you lose 30% from killing Kitava.
      • Put every armor and jewelry piece you have into the crafting bench in your hideout to see if it has space for a Fire, Cold, or Lightning resist craft. This is only usable if the item has an Open Suffix (hold Alt on an item to see). 
    • Complete the quest Return to Oriath and talk to Lani for a ring matching the resists you need the most. Get any of the Two-Stones over the single-resist rings, as they are easier to replace later.
    • Complete the quests In Service to Science, The Key to Freedom, and reach the Chamber of Innocence
    Step 2: Level 38
    • Once you enter the Chamber of Innocence, farm this zone until at least level 40. You can reset a campaign zone by holding down CTRL and clicking on the zone or Waypoint.
    • If you are clearing the Chamber of Innocence and are still under level 40, use a Portal Scroll and talk to Lani for a Jade Flask.
    • Talk to Vilenta for a Passive Skillpoint.
    • Complete the quest Death to Purity.
    Step 3: Level 40
    • Complete the quests The Ravenous God and Kitava's Torments.
    • When Kitava closes his fist, he will spawn orbs that hone in on your location in an X formation, exploding in the center. Avoid any slams and abilities from the large unique monsters that spawn. Kill Kitava, and move onto Act six.
    • After killing Kitava, your resists drop by 30%. This should be easy to overcome, and in softcore your resists should be at least 50% each. Every point of resistance gear from here on out drastically increases your survivability!
    Step 1: Level 42
    • If you are 42, you don't need to farm any XP past this point until Act 9, just be within 6 levels of the zone.
    • Entering this Act without 75% all resists is perfectly fine (in softcore!). The elemental damage you take here depends on which part of the Act you are in. The Mud Flats and the Tukohama fight requires Fire res, the Shavronne fight Lightning res, and the Brine King Cold and Lightning res. Using this, it’s okay if your resists initially look like 75% Fire, 50% Cold, and 50% Lightning (again, in softcore). In hardcore, ensure that all resists equal 75% or more.
    • Go to your hideout and throw each piece individually at the crafting bench to see if you can craft a Fire, Cold, or Lightning resist on them for 1 Orb of Transmutation each. Obviously only craft the types of resists you need!
    • Go out to The Coast and get the Waypoint, then go to the Mud Flats and complete the quest The Father of War.
    • Once at the Ridge Waypoint, take it back to The Coast and complete Bestel's Epic.
    • Use a Portal Scroll, complete Fallen From Grace, and return to town.
    Step 2
    • Talk to Lily Roth for 2x Passive Respec Points. Purchase Increased Area of Effect Support because you will likely not have the Intelligence required for a higher level version from Act 4.
    • Additionally, purchase Flame Dash to replace Smoke Mine.
    • Talk to Bestel for either a rare Leather Belt or Agate Amulet, depending on which slot needs a bigger upgrade.
    • Talk to Tarkleigh for a Passive Skillpoint.
    • Travel to The Lower Prison and complete the Trial of Ascendancy there.
    • Finish the Essence of Umbra by killing Brutus and Shavronne.
    Step 3
    • Kill Abberath in Prisoner's Gate for The Cloven One.
    • Grab the Waypoint in The Riverways, then go north to The Wetlands and kill Ryslatha for The Puppet Mistress.
      • You may skip Abberath and Ryslatha until late Act 7 if your character is too weak at this point.
    • Return to town, then talk to Bestel for a Passive Skillpoint.
    • Talk to Tarkleigh for a Fencer Helm and a Passive Skillpoint.
    • Complete the remaining questline to defeat The Brine King, then move onto Act 7.
    Step 1
    • Select the Brine King Pantheon (the Keybind is Y by default)
    • Complete the quest The Silver Locket
    • Travel to The Crypt for the Trial of Ascendancy and the map for Essence of the Artist.
    • Waypoint back to The Crossroads, travel north to the Chamber of Sins, and complete Web of Secrets.
    • Do the Trial of Ascendancy while in the Chambers of Sin 2, and complete The Master of a Million Faces.
    Step 2
    • Optionally, you may travel back to town at this point and talk to Helena for an Amulet reward.
    • Gather the seven fireflies for the quest Lighting the Way, and kill Greust for the quest The Master of a Million Faces while in The Dread Thicket.
    • Exit The Dread Thicket and travel to The Causeway for Kishara's Star.
    • Get to the Waypoint in the Vaal City and talk to Yeena there, then return to town.
      • This also frees your inventory of the fireflies!
    Step 3
    • Select the Pantheon Ralakesh, Master of a Million Faces.
    • Talk to Eramir for two Passive Skillpoints.
    • Talk to Weylam Roth to select the Quartz Flask and a Passive Skillpoint.
    • Talk to Helena and select the Carnal Boots.
    • After this, complete the Cruel Labyrinth and choose Master Toxicist.
      • Master Toxicist causes any poison you apply to have a 20% chance to double in damage, greatly helping your DPS. Additionally, the Poison "Proliferation" on kill effect will smooth out any issues you may have with clearing. Note that this Proliferation and chance to deal double poison damage does not affect Plague Bearer.
    Step 4
    • Finally, Waypoint back to The Vaal City, travel to Temple of Decay 2, and kill Arakaali. Avoid the cone of poisonous bolts, as well as the purple orbs that spawn far away and travel towards the boss. After this, move onto Act 8!
    Step 1
    • Kill Doedre for the quest Essence of the Hag, then go to the right towards The Quay.
    • Complete the quest Love is Dead, then travel to the Grain Gate to complete The Gemling Legion.

After this, continue onwards to Act 9 and the best zone in the campaign for farming - the Blood Aqueducts!

Summary

Mirage 3.28
Viper Strike of the Mamba Pathfinder League Starter
  • The Viper Strike of the Mamba Pathfinder is a League Start Poison build with good clear speed and defense.
  • Viper Strike of the Mamba achieves maximum damage without a ramp-up, resulting in a quicker playstyle.
  • The Pathfinder Ascendancy notable, Master Toxicist, circumvents the single poison limitation of Viper Strike of the Mamba by allowing poison to spread to nearby enemies.
  • This poison spread scales multiplicatively with density, enabling efficient clearing of monster packs and stronger targets within them in mechanics such as Breach, Expedition, Ritual, Essence, Blight, and Harvest.
  • Early itemization focuses on hybrid scaling of Life, Energy Shield, and Evasion for enhanced survivability.
  • Master Surgeon provides continuous life recovery, providing huge recovery and mitigating the downside of Petrified Blood.While the build does not need unique items, The Sorrow of the Divine extends life flask effects to Energy Shield, doubling recovery.
