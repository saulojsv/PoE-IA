# PoE Wiki mechanics study protocol

Start with the [PoE Wiki game mechanics index](https://www.poewiki.net/wiki/Game_mechanics) and follow links for the exact mechanic, skill, item, passive, ailment, damage conversion, defense, recovery, or trigger under test. Use the [Stat](https://www.poewiki.net/wiki/Stat) page when a mechanic depends on stat application or calculation order.

For every page used, record:

- URL, access date, game patch/version and exact claim;
- mechanic category and dependencies;
- the corresponding PoB tab/field (Tree, Skills, Items, Config, Calcs or Notes);
- expected direction and conditions;
- one-variable test, interaction test, result, and confidence;
- conflicts with PoB, official sources, or other evidence.

Wiki content is a discovery and explanation source. PoB output, official patch data, and controlled tests decide whether a rule is accepted. Keep version-specific and conditional mechanics separate; do not generalize from popularity or one build.
